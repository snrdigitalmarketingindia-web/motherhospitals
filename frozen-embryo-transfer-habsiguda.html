FAQs — Habsiguda Patients

How is FET done near Habsiguda?
FET (Frozen Embryo Transfer) involves thawing your frozen embryos and transferring them to your prepared uterine lining. The transfer procedure takes about 5 minutes — no anaesthesia, similar to a Pap smear. Lining preparation takes 2–3 weeks before transfer day.
What is the FET success rate near Habsiguda?
FET success rates at Mother Hospitals are comparable to fresh embryo transfer — and often better in women who had OHSS risk or suboptimal lining during the fresh cycle. Blastocyst FET has particularly high success rates.
Can I do FET at Mother Hospitals if my embryos are frozen elsewhere?
Yes — embryos can be transported from another clinic to Mother Hospitals near Habsiguda for FET. Dr. Prashanthi regularly performs FET for patients whose embryos were frozen at other Hyderabad clinics. Contact us to arrange embryo transport.
When is ERA needed for FET near Habsiguda?
ERA is recommended after 2+ failed FETs with good-quality embryos. It identifies if your endometrial receptivity window is at a different time than standard — changing transfer timing by 12–24 hours, with significant improvement in success rates.
