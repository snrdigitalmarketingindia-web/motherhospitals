Optional Add-ons

When Additional Tests May Be Recommended

The following are only recommended when clinically indicated — they are never routine upsells:

🧬 PGT-A Genetic Testing

Preimplantation Genetic Testing for Aneuploidy — screens embryos for chromosomal abnormalities before transfer. Recommended for recurrent miscarriage, advanced maternal age (>38), or repeated implantation failure.

🔬 ERA (Endometrial Receptivity Test)

Identifies the personalised implantation window. Recommended after 2+ failed transfers with good-quality embryos.

❄️ Embryo Freezing (Vitrification)

If surplus embryos are produced, vitrification for future FET cycles. Recommended for all patients with extra embryos — avoids the cost of repeat stimulation.

🧪 Semen DNA Fragmentation (DFI) Test

Checks sperm DNA damage — important for unexplained infertility, recurrent miscarriage, or poor fertilisation history.

🔩 TESA / PESA

Surgical sperm retrieval for azoospermia (no sperm in semen). Required for obstructive or non-obstructive azoospermia patients.

💊 Needleless IVF Protocol

Oral medication protocol instead of daily injections. Available at no extra charge at Mother Hospitals — included in the IVF package for eligible patients.
