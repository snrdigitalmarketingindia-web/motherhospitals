What Affects IVF Cost?

What's Included (₹99,000)

Stimulation injections, egg pick-up with anaesthesia, ICSI fertilisation, embryo culture, embryo transfer, all monitoring ultrasound scans, and all consultations throughout the cycle. For a full cost breakdown and what to watch for when comparing clinics, read our complete IVF cost guide for Hyderabad 2026.

What May Cost Extra

Additional tests (ERA endometrial receptivity, PGT-A genetic testing), TESA/PESA for male infertility, frozen embryo storage, frozen embryo transfer (FET) in a subsequent cycle, and advanced add-ons like Laser Assisted Hatching.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the ₹99,000 IVF package really all-inclusive?+

Yes. Our package includes stimulation injections, OPU (egg pick-up), ICSI, embryo culture, embryo transfer, and all consultations and monitoring scans — with no hidden charges. Valid till 30 June 2026.

What is the cost of a second IVF cycle?+

If the first cycle does not succeed, a second cycle at Mother Hospitals is available at a discounted rate. Please contact us for details as pricing may vary based on your protocol.

Are fertility medications included in the IVF cost?+

Yes — stimulation injections are included in our ₹99,000 all-inclusive package. This is a key differentiator as many clinics charge ₹30,000–50,000 extra for medications.

Is there EMI/finance available for IVF?+

We can help guide you on IVF loan options through partner financial institutions. Please ask our counsellor during your consultation.

Does insurance cover IVF in India?+

Some new private health insurance plans have started covering fertility treatments. We can provide all documentation needed for insurance claims. Government schemes like Aarogyasri do not currently cover IVF.

What is the cost of IUI compared to IVF?+

IUI is significantly more affordable than IVF — typically ₹5,000–15,000 per cycle depending on whether fertility drugs are used. It is always worth trying IUI first if medically appropriate.
