Our Services

🥚 Who Needs Donor Egg IVF?

Premature ovarian failure. Multiple failed IVF cycles with own eggs. Very low AMH (under 0.3). Genetic disorders in own eggs. Age 43+ with poor egg quality. Donor eggs bypass the egg quality barrier while you carry the pregnancy and deliver.

🔬 The Process Explained

Anonymous donor (ART Act 2021 screened) undergoes stimulation. Eggs retrieved. Fertilised with husband's sperm via ICSI in our lab. Best embryo(s) transferred to your prepared uterus. Progesterone support for 2 weeks. Pregnancy test on Day 14.

📊 60–70% Success Rate

Donor eggs come from young, healthy donors — peak egg quality. Success rate per transfer: 60–70%, significantly higher than own-egg IVF for women over 38. Frozen embryo transfers from the same retrieval also available — multiple attempts from one cycle.

Can a working woman manage donor egg IVF without taking too much leave?
Yes. As the recipient, you take hormone tablets/patches (no injections needed typically). Monitoring scans: 2–3 visits. Embryo transfer day: half day off. Total leave needed: 2–3 days spread over 6 weeks. Very manageable for IT professionals.
Will the baby look like me?
The baby will have the donor's egg genetics and your husband's sperm genetics. Physical resemblance depends on how closely the donor was matched to you. We match blood group, height, and general physical characteristics within ART Act 2021 guidelines.
What if the first transfer fails?
If frozen embryos are available, a second FET (Frozen Embryo Transfer) can be attempted 1–2 months later at a fraction of the original cost. If no embryos remain, a new donor cycle may be needed. Dr. Prashanthi reviews every case personally.
Is donor egg IVF legal in India?
Yes — completely legal under ART (Regulation) Act 2021. The Act defines donor eligibility, anonymity, consent protocols, and recipient rights. Mother Hospitals is ART Act 2021 certified and follows all regulations strictly.
