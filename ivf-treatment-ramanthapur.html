📊 Success Rates — Honest Numbers

Under 35: 55–65% per cycle. 35–38: 40–50%. 38–40: 30–40%. Donor egg: 60–70%. Dr. Prashanthi discusses realistic chances based on your age, AMH, and diagnosis at the very first consultation — no overpromising.

🌿 Needleless IVF Option

Oral tablet-based stimulation — no daily injections. Same lab, same doctor, same success for suitable patients. Popular with working professionals and those uncomfortable with injections. Ask Dr. Prashanthi if you qualify.

❄️ Frozen Embryo Transfer

Extra embryos cryopreserved for future use. FET cycle costs a fraction of fresh IVF. Frozen embryo transfer success rates match fresh — sometimes better. One retrieval cycle can give multiple transfer attempts.

🧪 PGT-A — Genetic Testing

Embryo biopsy at Day 5. All 24 chromosomes tested. Only genetically normal embryo transferred. Miscarriage rate drops from 30%+ to under 5%. Recommended for 38+, recurrent failure, recurrent miscarriage.

Frequently Asked Questions

How far is Mother Hospitals from Ramanthapur?
~5 km via Uppal Road — approximately 10 minutes by car or 12–15 minutes by auto. Landmark: Aakruthi Township, Boduppal. Very close to Uppal X Roads. Easy and quick commute for monitoring visits.
What is the first step to start IVF from Ramanthapur?
Call 97059 93366 and book a fertility assessment — AMH blood test, antral follicle count scan, and semen analysis. This tells us your ovarian reserve, ovulation status, and husband's sperm quality. Takes 1–2 hours. After results, Dr. Prashanthi plans your IVF protocol.
Do I need to bring my previous reports?
Yes — please bring any fertility investigations, previous IVF records, semen analysis reports, and relevant medical history. If you don't have them, we can arrange fresh tests in the same visit.
Can I start IVF this month?
Often yes, if you consult before Day 3 of your current period. IVF stimulation starts on Day 2–3. If you just missed the window, next cycle start — usually 4–6 weeks away. Dr. Prashanthi will confirm after reviewing your reports.
Is there an age limit for IVF at Mother Hospitals?
For own-egg IVF: up to age 45 (case by case). For donor egg IVF: up to age 50 under ART Act 2021. Dr. Prashanthi reviews uterine health and overall fitness — age alone is not the deciding factor.
