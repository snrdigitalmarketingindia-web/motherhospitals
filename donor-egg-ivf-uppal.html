Our Services

🥚 When is Donor Egg IVF Recommended?

Premature ovarian failure / menopause. Multiple failed IVF cycles with own eggs. Very low AMH (under 0.3). Genetic conditions in own eggs. Age 43+ with poor egg quality. If own eggs cannot produce viable embryos, donor eggs offer the best path to pregnancy.

🔬 How Donor Egg IVF Works

Carefully matched anonymous donor (ART Act 2021 compliant) undergoes stimulation + egg retrieval. Eggs fertilised with husband's sperm via ICSI. Best-quality embryo(s) transferred to your uterus, prepared with hormone support. Your body carries the pregnancy.

📊 Success Rates

Donor egg IVF success: 60–70% per transfer — significantly higher than own-egg IVF for women over 40, because donor eggs are typically from women aged 21–30 in peak health. Multiple embryos can be frozen for subsequent attempts.

⚖️ ART Act 2021 Compliance

India's ART Act 2021 mandates strict donor anonymity, medical screening, and legal consent protocols. Mother Hospitals is ART Act 2021 certified — all donor programs follow government regulations. Donors are anonymous; legal parentage belongs to the recipient couple.

Frequently Asked Questions

Will my baby genetically be mine with donor eggs?
The baby will have husband's genetics (sperm) and the donor's genetics (egg) — not your own genetics. However, you carry the pregnancy, deliver, breastfeed, and raise the child. Many parents find the bond of pregnancy deeply meaningful regardless of genetics.
How is the donor matched?
ART Act 2021 mandates anonymous donation. We match on blood group, physical characteristics (height, complexion, build). Donors undergo full medical screening — genetics, infectious diseases, psychological evaluation. You will not know the donor's identity.
How long does the donor egg IVF process take from Uppal?
Process starts after consultation. Donor matching: 2–4 weeks. Donor stimulation cycle: 10–14 days. Embryo transfer: after your uterus is prepared (2–3 weeks). Total: 6–8 weeks from first appointment. You visit the clinic 4–5 times total.
Can I freeze extra embryos?
Yes. Multiple quality embryos formed can be vitrified (frozen) for future transfers. Frozen embryo transfer (FET) is much less expensive than a fresh cycle and has comparable success rates.
Is there an age limit for recipient?
Indian law under ART Act 2021 allows recipient women up to age 50 for donor egg IVF. Dr. Prashanthi will review your uterine health and overall fitness before proceeding.
