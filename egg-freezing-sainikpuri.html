Our Services

❄️ Vitrification — Gold Standard

Flash-freezing with vitrification prevents ice-crystal damage to eggs. Thaw survival rate: 80–90%. Quality preserved indefinitely. Mother Hospitals uses vitrification for all egg and embryo freezing — the most reliable method available.

📅 Best Age to Freeze

28–34: ideal. Good AMH, best egg quality, fewest cycles needed. 35–38: still effective, may need more eggs. 38+: possible but results depend heavily on AMH and AFC. Dr. Prashanthi checks your reserve before advising.

🔢 How Many Eggs to Freeze?

Under 35: aim for 10–15 mature eggs (70–80% live birth chance). 35–38: 15–20 eggs. Multiple cycles can be combined. Dr. Prashanthi sets a personalised target based on your AMH and AFC.

🏥 Cancer & Medical Freezing

Chemotherapy and radiation can damage fertility permanently. Egg freezing before treatment preserves your future options. Emergency appointments available for oncology patients. We coordinate quickly with your oncologist.

⏱️ Low Clinic Visits Required

Egg freezing: stimulation monitoring 3–4 scans over 10–14 days. Retrieval day: sedation, 20 min procedure, 2 hrs recovery. Done. From Sainikpuri: totally manageable with morning slots.

⚖️ ART Act 2021 Storage

India allows 5-year storage (extendable). Vitrified eggs maintain quality indefinitely. Single women can freeze eggs without a partner — used with partner's or donor sperm when ready. Fully legal under ART Act 2021.

I'm posted here for 2 years — is it safe to freeze eggs and move?
Yes — frozen eggs can be stored at Mother Hospitals for 5+ years. Wherever you are later, we coordinate the thaw and IVF cycle when you're ready. Your eggs don't age; they stay at today's quality, indefinitely.
Does egg freezing affect natural fertility later?
No. Frozen eggs are ones that would have been lost naturally that cycle anyway. Your remaining follicles, ovulation, and future fertility are unaffected. You can freeze eggs this year and still try naturally next year.

Is egg freezing painful?
