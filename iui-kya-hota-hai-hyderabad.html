ज़रूरी बात

IUI kya hota hai aur kiske liye kaam karta hai?

IUI (Intrauterine Insemination) — husband ke sperm ko wash karke directly uterus mein dala jaata hai. Fallopian tubes open hone par, mild male factor mein, unexplained infertility mein — IUI pehla try karte hain. IVF se bahut kam cost, process simple. 20–30% success per cycle. Call: 97059 93366.

हमारी Services

💉 IUI Process Kaise Hota Hai?

Pehle ovulation track karte hain (ultrasound + blood test). Ovulation ke time husband ka sperm collect karke lab mein wash karte hain. Fine catheter se directly uterus mein daala jaata hai. Sirf 15 minute — koi dard nahi, anaesthesia nahi. Usi din ghar ja sakte hain.

🎯 IUI Kiske Liye Suitable Hai?

Tubes open hain to. Husband sperm count mildly kam ho to. 1–2 saal try karne ke baad bhi pregnancy na ho (unexplained). PCOS mein ovulation stimulate karne ke baad. Cervical mucus problem mein.

💰 IUI Cost Kitni Hai?

IUI cost IVF se bahut kam hoti hai. Medicated IUI (tablets + monitoring + IUI) — affordable price par Mother Hospitals mein. Exact quote ke liye 97059 93366 call karein.

📊 IUI Success Rate Kitna?

Natural IUI: 10–15% per attempt. Medicated IUI (stimulation ke saath): 20–30%. 3 cycles try karte hain — kaam na kare to IVF ki taraf badhte hain. Umar kam ho to success better.

🔄 Kitne IUI Cycles Karne Chahiye?

3 cycles try karte hain normally. Teesre mein bhi kaam na ho to IVF better option. Dr. Prashanthi har cycle ke baad review karti hain aur honestly guide karti hain.

💊 Medicated IUI Kya Hai?

IUI ke saath ovaries stimulate karne ke liye tablets (letrozole/clomiphene) dete hain. Isse 1–2 follicles better develop hote hain. Success rate natural IUI se better hoti hai.

अक्सर पूछे जाने वाले Questions

IUI mein dard hota hai kya?
Nahi — IUI bahut mild procedure hai. Catheter andar jaate waqt thodi si cramping feel ho sakti hai — period jaise. Anaesthesia nahi chahiye. Procedure ke baad 15–20 minute rest karke ghar ja sakte hain.
IUI ke baad kab pregnancy test karein?
IUI ke 14 din baad blood pregnancy test (beta-hCG) karwayein. Urine home test 14 din se pehle accurate nahi hoti. Blood test zyada reliable hai — yahi karte hain hum.
IUI fail ho to seedha IVF?
Teen IUI cycles fail hone ke baad IVF discuss karte hain. Lekin agar age 35+, tubes band hain, ya severe male factor hai — to IUI skip karke seedha IVF better. Dr. Prashanthi aapki specific situation dekh ke honest advice deti hain.
Husband sperm count kitna ho to IUI kar sakte hain?
Wash ke baad minimum 5–10 million motile sperm chahiye IUI ke liye. Isse kam ho (severe male factor) to IVF+ICSI better option. Semen analysis report dekh ke Dr. Prashanthi clearly bata deti hain.
IUI cycle kitne time mein complete hota hai?
Period se lekar 3–4 hafte. Day 2–3 baseline scan → 5–7 din tablets → monitoring scan → ovulation trigger → IUI procedure → 14 din baad pregnancy test. Simple schedule — zyada clinic visits nahi chahiye.
