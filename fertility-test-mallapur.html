What We Offer — Serving Mallapur Patients

🧬 AMH Test Near Mallapur

Anti-Müllerian Hormone — the best single measure of ovarian reserve. Can be done on any day of the cycle. Critical before IVF.

📅 Day 2 Hormone Panel Near Mallapur

FSH, LH, estradiol, prolactin — done on Day 2–3 of the menstrual cycle. Assesses pituitary-ovarian axis function.

🔬 Antral Follicle Count (AFC) Near Mallapur

Transvaginal ultrasound on Day 2–5 counts the antral follicles in both ovaries — reflects available egg pool.

🦋 Thyroid & Prolactin Near Mallapur

FAQs — Mallapur Patients

What fertility tests should I have near Mallapur?
For women: AMH, Day 2 FSH/LH/estradiol, AFC ultrasound, thyroid, prolactin. For men: semen analysis. Dr. Prashanthi recommends the appropriate tests for your specific situation.
What is a good AMH level?
AMH interpretation depends on age: at 25–30, above 2 ng/mL is good. At 31–35, above 1.5 ng/mL. At 36–40, above 0.7 ng/mL. Below 1.0 ng/mL at any age suggests reduced reserve.
Can I get a fertility test without seeing a doctor?
Dr. Prashanthi recommends having results interpreted in context — a number without context can cause unnecessary anxiety or false reassurance.
What does a normal semen analysis look like?
WHO 2021: count above 16 million/mL, progressive motility above 30%, morphology above 4%. Below these thresholds suggests male factor — though ICSI can overcome even very poor parameters.
