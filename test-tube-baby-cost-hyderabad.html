⚠️ What May Be Additional

The following are not part of the base package and may be required depending on your specific clinical case. All additional costs are communicated clearly before treatment begins — no surprises.

→
Injectable stimulation medicines — if you require conventional gonadotropin injections (rather than Needleless IVF oral medications), medication costs depend on your required dose and duration (typically ₹20,000–60,000).
→
Frozen Embryo Transfer (FET) — if a fresh transfer is not possible (e.g. OHSS risk, uterine lining issues) or you return for a second embryo in a later cycle, FET is charged separately.

TESA / PESA — surgical sperm retrieval for azoospermia (zero sperm count) cases. Requires a separate procedure and charges.
→
Pre-cycle blood tests — initial baseline investigations (AMH, FSH, LH, semen analysis, infection screening) done before the IVF cycle begins are charged at standard rates.

Cost Factors

Factors That Affect Your IVF Cost

While our IVF package covers a standard IVF cycle, your final cost may vary based on your individual clinical situation. Here are the key factors that can influence the total cost of test tube baby treatment.

👩 Your Age & Ovarian Reserve

Older women or those with low AMH may require higher doses of stimulation medications (if not on Needleless IVF), which increases the medicine cost. Age also affects the likelihood of success per cycle — if multiple cycles are needed, total cost increases.

🔄 Number of IVF Cycles

Most couples achieve pregnancy in 1–3 cycles. If a second or third cycle is needed, each fresh cycle is priced identically to the first cycle. However, if frozen embryos remain from the first cycle, a Frozen Embryo Transfer (FET) is significantly cheaper than a fresh cycle.

💊 Stimulation Medications

The biggest variable in IVF cost is injectable stimulation medicines. Medication doses depend on your ovarian reserve and response. Needleless IVF (oral medications) at Mother Hospitals eliminates or significantly reduces this cost for many patients.

🧬 Additional Procedures

PGT-A genetic testing, TESA/PESA for male factor, ERA (Endometrial Receptivity Array) test for recurrent implantation failure, or Laser Assisted Hatching may be recommended in specific clinical cases, each adding to the total cost.
