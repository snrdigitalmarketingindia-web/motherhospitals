💊 Medical Management

🏥 Fertility-Preserving Surgery

FAQs — Habsiguda Patients

Why is endometriosis common in IT women from Habsiguda?
Delayed childbearing, high stress, irregular sleep, and sedentary work patterns — common in IT and research roles near Habsiguda — are associated with increased endometriosis severity. Women who delay pregnancy past 30–35 give endometriosis more time to progress and damage fertility.
What are signs of endometriosis near Habsiguda?
Severely painful periods (much worse than normal), pain during intercourse, chronic pelvic pain, heavy periods, pain during bowel movements, and unexplained infertility. Some women have no pain at all — endometriosis is only found during infertility investigation.
Should I have surgery or IVF for endometriosis near Habsiguda?
Depends on stage: Mild (Stage 1–2) — go straight to IVF. Moderate–severe (Stage 3–4) with large cysts — surgery first may improve outcomes. Large chocolate cysts — freeze eggs first, then surgery. Dr. Prashanthi plans case by case based on your AMH, age, and cyst size.
Does endometriosis surgery affect fertility near Habsiguda?
Paradoxically, chocolate cyst surgery can reduce ovarian reserve (AMH) by removing healthy follicle-containing tissue along with the cyst. This is why Dr. Prashanthi often recommends egg freezing BEFORE surgery — to bank eggs at their best quality before any procedure.
