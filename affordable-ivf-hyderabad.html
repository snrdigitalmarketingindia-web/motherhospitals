How We Do It

How Do We Keep IVF Affordable Without Compromise?

Operational Efficiency

Streamlined protocols, efficient scheduling, and experienced staff who don't over-investigate or over-prescribe reduce costs — and we pass those savings to you directly.

Medication Procurement

We source medications at institutional rates and pass the benefit to patients. Our all-inclusive model means we have no incentive to over-medicate.

Right-sized Protocols

Not every patient needs the same stimulation. Our individualised approach avoids unnecessary high-dose medication that inflates costs without improving outcomes.

No Commission Structure

We don't pay referral commissions to diagnostic labs or pharmacies, and our doctors are salaried — not incentivised to order unnecessary tests or procedures.

Questions to Ask Any IVF Clinic About Pricing

Before committing to a clinic, ask these questions to avoid bill-shock later. We answer all of them upfront at Mother Hospitals.
