Payment Options

All the Ways You Can Pay for IVF

Credit Card EMI

Most major bank credit cards support converting large medical bills to EMI. HDFC, ICICI, SBI, Axis, Kotak, and IDFC First Bank cards are widely accepted. Some banks offer zero-cost EMI for healthcare — check with your bank before your visit.

In-House Payment Plan

For patients without a credit card, we offer an in-house payment arrangement. Pay a portion upfront and the balance in agreed monthly instalments directly to the hospital. Requires government ID and address proof only.

Medical Financing Partners

We partner with medical lending institutions who specialise in fertility treatment loans. Available to salaried and self-employed individuals. Quick processing, minimal paperwork, and competitive rates.

UPI / NEFT / Cash

Full payment via UPI, bank transfer, or cash is always accepted. Patients paying in full upfront receive priority scheduling and — where applicable — a small processing fee waiver.

Financial Planning

Why Financial Planning for IVF Matters

IVF is not just one procedure — for some patients it takes 2–3 cycles. Planning your finances ahead avoids the stress of mid-treatment financial decisions.

  • Avoid mid-cycle financial pressure that affects mental health and outcomes
  • Plan for frozen embryo transfer cycles (₹30,000 each) in advance
  • Know exactly what you'll spend — no bill shock with our all-inclusive model
  • Preserve savings for early parenthood expenses after a successful cycle
  • Use existing insurance coverage where applicable (some group policies cover IVF)
  • EMI spreads cost across months rather than one large withdrawal
  • No-cost EMI options mean zero extra expense if your card qualifies
  • Our counsellors help you map total expected spend before you begin

FAQ

IVF EMI — Frequently Asked Questions

Can I pay for IVF in EMI in Hyderabad?
Yes. Mother Hospitals & IVF Center offers flexible EMI payment plans for IVF treatment in Hyderabad. Plans start from approximately ₹8,250 per month for a 12-month term on our all-inclusive IVF package.
What EMI plans are available for IVF?
We offer 3-month, 6-month, and 12-month EMI plans. For our standard IVF package: 3 months = ₹33,000/month; 6 months = ₹16,500/month; 12 months = approximately ₹8,250/month. Exact rates depend on your financing partner terms.
Is there a no-cost EMI option for IVF?
Select cards and financing partners offer zero-interest EMI for medical treatments. Ask our billing counsellor about current no-cost EMI offers applicable to your bank card at the time of your visit.
What documents are required for IVF EMI?
For in-house payment plans, minimal documentation is required — government ID and address proof. For third-party financing partners, a recent bank statement and income proof may be requested.
Can I pay IVF cost in parts without a credit card?
Yes. We work with medical financing partners who offer personal loans specifically for fertility treatments. These do not require a credit card and are available to salaried and self-employed individuals.
Does the IVF EMI plan cover all costs including medications?
Our all-inclusive package already covers all medications, consultations, scans, egg retrieval, embryology, and embryo transfer. The EMI plan covers this entire package — there are no separate medication bills.
What happens if my IVF cycle fails — do I still pay the EMI?
EMI payments are for the treatment services provided, not contingent on the outcome. However, our affordable Frozen Embryo Transfer (₹30,000) makes a second attempt accessible. We counsel all patients on this before treatment begins so you can plan holistically.
How do I apply for an IVF EMI plan at Mother Hospitals?
Call or WhatsApp us at +91 90520 74999 and ask to speak to our financial counsellor. They will explain all options, eligibility, and help you complete minimal paperwork. You can begin this process at your first consultation visit.
