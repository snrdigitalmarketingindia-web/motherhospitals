Our Services

💉 IUI — Simple Process

Tablet stimulation (Day 2–6) → monitoring scan Day 10–12 → HCG trigger injection when follicle ready → IUI procedure 36 hours later. Sperm washed in lab, inserted via fine catheter into uterus. 15 minutes. No anaesthesia. Home same day.

🎯 Who IUI Works For

Unexplained infertility (trying 1–2 years, all tests normal). Mild male factor (count 10–20M). PCOS — combined with ovulation induction. Cervical mucus issues. Single women using donor sperm.

💰 Cost — Much Less Than IVF

IUI is a fraction of IVF cost — fewer steps, fewer medicines, no egg retrieval procedure. Yet 20–30% success per cycle means many couples avoid IVF entirely. 3 cycles tried before IVF is considered.

📊 Success Rate

Natural IUI: 10–15%. Medicated IUI (letrozole + trigger): 20–30%. Age matters — under 35 best success. 3 failed IUIs → IVF recommended. Dr. Prashanthi advises honestly based on your specific profile.

🔁 IUI vs IVF — How to Choose?

If tubes are open, age under 37, sperm count reasonable — try IUI first. If tubes blocked, age 38+, severe male factor, failed 3 IUIs — IVF is right. Dr. Prashanthi discusses this at first consultation with your reports.

Frequently Asked Questions

How many days off work do I need for IUI near LB Nagar?
Very little. Monitoring scans: morning appointments, 30 minutes. IUI day: 1–2 hours total. You can go to work same day. Across a full cycle (3–4 weeks), you need maybe 3–4 morning appointments total.
Is IUI painful?
Minimal discomfort — similar to a Pap smear. Mild cramping as catheter passes through cervix. Settles in minutes. No anaesthesia. Most women go for lunch afterwards. Some feel mild cramping for an hour — normal.
Can I do IUI if I have PCOS?
Yes — IUI works well for PCOS. First, ovulation is induced with letrozole tablets. Scan confirms follicle development. Trigger injection at the right time. IUI timed to ovulation. 20–30% success per medicated IUI cycle for PCOS.
How far is Mother Hospitals from LB Nagar?
~14 km via Nagole → Uppal → Boduppal (NH65). Approximately 20 minutes by car in normal traffic. Alternatively: Hyderabad Metro to Uppal station → 5-minute auto to Boduppal. Very manageable for regular monitoring visits.
What if 3 IUIs fail?
After 3 unsuccessful IUI cycles, we review the case — check if any factor was missed, consider HSG (tube patency), repeat semen analysis. If no new finding, IVF is the next step. Most couples who eventually succeed do so within 3 IUI attempts.
