Egg Freezing in India 2026 — Complete Guide for Women

✍️ Dr. E. Prashanthi Reddy📅 2026-05-15⏱️ 8 min read
Egg FreezingFertility PreservationIndiaHyderabad

Egg freezing (oocyte cryopreservation) has transformed from an experimental procedure into a mainstream fertility preservation option. In 2026, more Indian women than ever are choosing to freeze their eggs — giving themselves the gift of reproductive choice. This guide explains everything you need to know.

What Changed: Modern vitrification technology has transformed egg freezing from a 20-30% survival rate to 80-90%+ survival rate. Eggs frozen today have excellent potential for use even 10-15 years later.

What is Egg Freezing?

Egg freezing involves stimulating the ovaries to produce multiple eggs, retrieving them, and storing them in a frozen state using vitrification (ultra-rapid freezing). When you are ready for pregnancy, the eggs are thawed, fertilised with sperm in the lab (IVF/ICSI), and the resulting embryo is transferred to the uterus.

Who Should Consider Freezing Eggs?

1. Women Prioritising Career or Education

Women aged 28-35 who are not ready for motherhood yet but want to preserve their best quality eggs before age-related decline accelerates.

2. Women Facing Medical Treatment

Cancer patients facing chemotherapy or radiation, which can damage ovarian function. Egg freezing before cancer treatment is called oncofertility preservation.

3. Women with Low AMH or Endometriosis

If you have been told your ovarian reserve is declining, egg freezing urgently preserves whatever eggs are available now before the reserve declines further.

4. Women with a Family History of Early Menopause

If your mother or sister experienced premature menopause, you may be at higher risk. Freezing eggs at 28-32 is a protective measure.

5. Women Undergoing Ovarian Surgery

Surgery for ovarian cysts or endometriosis can damage surrounding ovarian tissue. Freezing eggs before surgery preserves fertility.

The Best Age to Freeze Eggs

  • Under 30: Ideal — best egg quality and quantity, highest success rates
  • 30-35: Very good — still excellent egg quality for most women
  • 35-37: Good — act quickly; success rates begin to decline
  • Over 38: Possible but success rates lower — discuss realistic expectations with your doctor

How Egg Freezing Works — Step by Step

  1. Initial assessment: AMH, AFC scan, hormone panel (3 days)
  2. Stimulation: Daily hormone medication (injections or Needleless protocol) for 10-14 days
  3. Monitoring: Regular ultrasound scans to track follicle growth
  4. Trigger injection: Final injection to mature the eggs
  5. Egg retrieval: Minor procedure under sedation (30-45 minutes)
  6. Vitrification: Eggs are flash-frozen and stored in liquid nitrogen

How Many Eggs Should I Freeze?

  • Under 35: 10-15 eggs gives reasonable odds (70-80% of having at least one baby)
  • 35-37: 15-20 eggs recommended for similar odds
  • Over 38: More eggs needed — may require multiple stimulation cycles

Important: Not all frozen eggs will survive thawing, not all will fertilise, and not all embryos will result in pregnancy. Freeze as many as possible in your optimal age window.

Is Egg Freezing Safe?

Yes — the hormone stimulation is the same as used in IVF, which has been safely performed for 40+ years. Children born from frozen eggs have the same health outcomes as naturally conceived children, based on extensive research data.

The main risk is Ovarian Hyperstimulation Syndrome (OHSS) — which is rare and preventable with proper monitoring. At Mother Hospitals, we use conservative stimulation protocols to minimise OHSS risk.

Frequently Asked Questions

At what age should I freeze my eggs?

The ideal age is between 28 and 35. Earlier is better — younger eggs have better quality and higher success rates. Over 37, success rates begin to decline significantly.

How much does egg freezing cost in Hyderabad?

Egg freezing in Hyderabad costs approximately ₹80,000–₹1,50,000 for the complete cycle including medications, retrieval, and vitrification. Annual storage is ₹5,000–₹10,000. Contact Mother Hospitals for current pricing.

How long can frozen eggs be stored?

Eggs can be stored indefinitely in liquid nitrogen. There is no evidence of reduced viability over time, and pregnancies have been achieved from eggs stored for 10+ years.

Is egg freezing painful?

The stimulation injections are manageable for most patients. Egg retrieval is done under mild sedation — you will not feel pain during the procedure. Mild bloating and cramping post-retrieval resolve within a few days.

Can I freeze eggs if I have low AMH?

Yes, but the number of eggs retrieved may be lower. Egg freezing is especially urgent for women with low AMH — don't delay. Mother Hospitals specialises in egg freezing for poor responders.
