What Is NOT Included in Typical IVF Packages?

Many clinics advertise low base prices but add costs separately. Always ask if the following are included:

  • Stimulation injections: Often charged separately — the biggest variable cost (₹40,000–₹80,000)
  • ICSI: Some clinics charge this separately (₹15,000–₹25,000)
  • Embryo freezing: If you have surplus embryos, storage costs apply
  • ERA Test: If recommended for recurrent failure (₹15,000–₹25,000)
  • Laser Assisted Hatching: Often an add-on (₹8,000–₹15,000)
  • Anaesthesia charges: For the Egg retrieval procedure
  • Medicines post-transfer: Progesterone, etc.

Hidden Costs to Watch Out For in Hyderabad

A common complaint from IVF patients in Hyderabad is that the final bill is far higher than the quoted price. Here are red flags:

  • Base package price quotes "IVF from ₹60,000" — without injections
  • Separate charges for each monitoring scan
  • ICSI and embryo transfer listed as "optional add-ons"
  • Additional fees for day 3 vs day 5 embryo culture
  • Charges for "advanced culture media" or "special incubators"

IVF Add-On Costs Worth Considering

Embryo Freezing (Vitrification)

If you produce surplus high-quality embryos, freezing them is strongly recommended. Cost: ₹15,000–₹25,000 for vitrification + ₹5,000–₹10,000 per year for storage.

ERA Test

For patients with previous implantation failures. Identifies the exact window of implantation. Cost: ₹15,000–₹25,000.

Laser Assisted Hatching

A laser technique to help the embryo break out of its shell for better implantation. Recommended in older patients or poor-grade embryos. Cost: ₹8,000–₹12,000.

IVF Cost vs Success Rate — The Real Value Equation

The cheapest IVF clinic may not offer the best value. A successful pregnancy in 1 cycle at ₹99,000 is far better than 3 failed cycles at ₹60,000 each (= ₹1,80,000 spent for nothing).

When evaluating cost, consider: clinic success rate per age group, lab quality and embryology team experience, doctor's expertise with your specific condition, transparency of all-inclusive pricing, and patient reviews.

EMI Options for IVF in Hyderabad

At Mother Hospitals, we understand that IVF is a significant financial commitment. We work with patients to explore bank EMI options through credit card zero-interest schemes, medical loan facilities through partner finance companies, and phased payment plans where applicable.

Is IVF Covered by Insurance in India?

Currently, most government insurance schemes including Aarogyasri and EHS do not cover IVF. However, some private health insurance plans (Apollo Munich, HDFC Ergo, Niva Bupa) now offer fertility riders. Mother Hospitals can provide documentation for insurance claims.
