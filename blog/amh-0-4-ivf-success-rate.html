AMH 0.4 IVF Success Rate — What to Expect and How to Improve Your Chances

AMH 0.4 is very low but not hopeless. With DHEA priming and the right IVF protocol, women under 38 with AMH 0.4 can retrieve enough eggs for a good blastocyst. Here is the success rate data and exactly what Dr. Prashanthi Reddy does differently.

AMH 0.4 — Where Does It Fall?

AMH 0.4 ng/mL is classified as very low ovarian reserve — below the 10th percentile for women under 35, and below the 20th percentile for women in their late 30s. It indicates fewer eggs remaining than average for your age, but it is a step above AMH 0.2 in terms of available eggs per stimulated cycle.

Good news for AMH 0.4: Many women with this AMH level retrieve 3–7 eggs in an IVF cycle — enough to get 1–2 good-quality blastocysts if the protocol is right and age is under 38.

DHEA and AMH 0.4 — Does It Help?

Yes — DHEA supplementation is one of the most evidence-backed interventions for poor ovarian reserve. For AMH 0.4 specifically:

  • DHEA 25–75mg daily for 60–90 days before IVF has been shown to increase egg number by 30–50% in poor responders in multiple studies (Gleicher et al., 2010; Wiser et al., 2010)
  • Mechanism: DHEA is converted to androgens in the ovary, which prime follicles to respond better to FSH stimulation
  • CoQ10 600mg daily is added for mitochondrial energy support in eggs — particularly relevant for women over 35 with AMH 0.4
  • After 90 days of DHEA priming, re-check AMH — some patients see it rise to 0.6–0.8, which meaningfully changes the outlook

AMH 0.4 — IVF Protocol at Mother Hospitals

Antagonist Protocol (Most Common)

Standard approach: 150–225 IU FSH daily, GnRH antagonist from day 5–6 to prevent premature ovulation. This maximises egg number while protecting against OHSS. Trigger with HCG or dual trigger (HCG + Lupron). OPU on day 12–14.

Dual Stimulation (DuoStim)

For AMH 0.4 patients who want to accumulate more embryos — stimulation in both the follicular and luteal phase of the same cycle. Retrieves eggs twice in one month. Used selectively.

Needleless IVF

If injections are a barrier — oral/transdermal protocol can retrieve 2–5 eggs in AMH 0.4 patients. Works well as part of a banking strategy.

AMH 0.4 — Natural Conception Still Possible?

Yes — particularly for women under 35. AMH 0.4 means fewer eggs, not zero eggs. Natural conception requires only one egg to be released and fertilised. However, if you have been trying for 6+ months without success with AMH 0.4, the window is getting smaller and specialist consultation is recommended rather than waiting further.

Key Takeaway

AMH 0.4 is low — but with the right protocol (DHEA priming + antagonist IVF), most women under 38 retrieve enough eggs to get at least one good blastocyst. One good embryo is all you need.
