What Does "IVF Success Rate" Actually Mean?

The term "success rate" is often misunderstood. There are different ways clinics measure it:

  • Per cycle started: The percentage of IVF cycles that result in a live birth, out of all cycles initiated
  • Per egg retrieval: Success calculated only for cycles where eggs were successfully retrieved
  • Per embryo transfer: Success rate for cycles where at least one embryo was transferred
  • Clinical pregnancy rate: Percentage with a heartbeat confirmed on ultrasound (higher than live birth rate)

Always ask for the live birth rate per transfer — this is the most honest and meaningful metric.

IVF Success Rates by Age — Hyderabad 2026

Age is the single biggest factor affecting IVF success. Here is how success rates generally compare:

  • Under 35 years: 50–65% success rate per transfer (best outcomes)
  • 35–37 years: 40–50% per transfer
  • 38–40 years: 30–40% per transfer
  • 41–42 years: 20–30% per transfer (donor eggs may be recommended)
  • Over 43 years: 10–20% with own eggs; significantly higher with donor eggs

Factors That Affect IVF Success in Hyderabad

1. Ovarian Reserve (AMH & AFC)

Anti-Müllerian Hormone (AMH) and Antral Follicle Count (AFC) indicate how many eggs your ovaries can produce. Low AMH doesn't prevent IVF from working — but it requires a specialist-tailored stimulation protocol. At Mother Hospitals, we offer dedicated Low AMH IVF treatment protocols.

2. Sperm Quality

Male factor infertility accounts for 40-50% of all infertility cases. ICSI (Intracytoplasmic Sperm Injection), TESA, and PESA procedures at Mother Hospitals address even severe male factor cases including azoospermia.

3. Embryo Quality & Lab Standards

The IVF laboratory is the heart of any IVF programme. At Mother Hospitals, our embryology team works in a controlled, state-of-the-art environment to maximise fertilisation and embryo development rates.

4. Uterine Health

A thin endometrium, polyps, fibroids, or adhesions can reduce implantation success. The ERA Test (Endometrial Receptivity Assay) available at Mother Hospitals can identify the ideal implantation window — especially valuable for patients with recurrent IVF failure.

5. Lifestyle Factors

Smoking, obesity, extreme stress, and poor diet all negatively impact IVF success. A pre-cycle consultation with Dr. Prashanthi Reddy includes specific lifestyle guidance for your situation.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the IVF success rate in Hyderabad?

IVF success rates in Hyderabad range from 40–65%+ per transfer, depending on age, AMH levels, sperm quality, and clinic expertise. Women under 35 with good ovarian reserve have the highest success rates.

How many IVF cycles are usually needed?

Many patients achieve pregnancy in 1-2 cycles. However, some require 3 or more cycles. Your doctor will reassess the protocol after each cycle to optimise outcomes.

Does low AMH mean IVF won't work?

No. Low AMH means fewer eggs may be retrieved, but IVF can still be successful with even 1-2 good quality eggs. Mother Hospitals offers specialised Low AMH IVF protocols.

What is the IVF success rate for 40-year-old women?

For women aged 40-42, the success rate is approximately 20-30% per transfer with their own eggs. Using donor eggs significantly increases the chances to 50-60%+.
