IVF ki Vellakamundu Yavai Tests Chesukuntaru?

Mother Hospitals lo, IVF consultation ki muнди Dr. Prashanthi Reddy following tests recommend chestundi. Ivi "IVF workup" ani call chestaru:

1. AMH Test (Anti-Müllerian Hormone)

Enti: Ovarian reserve — meeru enni eggs unnayo estimate chestundi.
Ela: Simple blood test. Cycle lo edaina day cheyavachu.
Results: Normal range: 1.5–4.0 ng/mL. Takkuva unte (below 1.0) — tailored IVF protocol kavali. Zyadha unte (PCOS) — OHSS risk manage chesaali.
Ikkade: Mother Hospitals lo cheyavachu — same day result.

2. Semen Analysis (Husband)

Enti: Sperm count, motility (swimming ability), morphology (shape) check.
Ela: 3–5 days abstinence tarvata sample provide cheyyadam. Lab lo 2 hours lo result.
Important: IVF success lo 50% male factor — husband tests mandatory, optional kaadu.
Avoid: Online lab reports ni trust cheyyakuindi — good andrology lab lo chesukuindi.

3. Pelvic Ultrasound (AFC — Antral Follicle Count)

Enti: Cycle day 2–4 lo transvaginal ultrasound — ovaries lo small follicles count. Idi AMH blood test confirm chestundi.
Bonus: Uterus shape, fibroids, polyps, ovarian cysts kuda check avutundi.
Important: Uterine cavity abnormalities IVF cheyyakamundu fix cheyyaali — hysteroscopy tho.

4. Hormonal Blood Panel (Day 2–3)

FSH, LH, Estradiol (E2), Prolactin, TSH (thyroid), Vitamin D.
Why: High FSH poor reserve indicate chestundi. High prolactin ovulation block chestundi. Thyroid issues IVF success affect chestaayi. Vitamin D deficiency implantation affect chestundi — correct chesukuinte better outcomes.

5. Infectious Disease Screening (Both Partners)

HIV, Hepatitis B, Hepatitis C, VDRL (syphilis).
Why: ART Act 2021 mandatory requirement — IVF start cheyyakamundu both partners clear avvaaali. Results negative unte process proceed avutundi.
