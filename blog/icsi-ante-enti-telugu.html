ICSI Ante Enti?

ICSI antే Intracytoplasmic Sperm Injection. Telugu lo artham: oka sperm ni direct ga egg loki inject cheyyatam.

Saadharana IVF lo, sperm and egg ni lab lo kalipataaru — sperm itself egg loki velthundi. Kaani ICSI lo, embryologist oka best sperm ni choose chesi, fine needle tho mature egg loki direct ga inject chestadu. Idi lab technology — patient ki ela feel avudadhu.
