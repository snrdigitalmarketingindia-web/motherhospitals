PCOS Unte Pregnant Avvadam Possible aa?

Yes — definitely possible. PCOS (ippudu PMOS ani rename chesaaru — May 2026 lo) most common cause of ovulatory infertility. Kaani idi treatable. Correct treatment tho:

  • 60–70% PCOS patients — ovulation induction tho conceive chestaru (no other fertility issues unte)
  • IUI tho additional 15–20% success
  • IVF tho 50–60% success rate under 35

PCOS lo Problem Enti?

PCOS lo main problem — ovulation sarigaa jarakatam ledu. Eggs grow start avutaayi, kaani release avvatam ledu. LH surge sarigaa radu — adi egg release trigger chestundi. Adi lekapothe, egg fallopian tube loki vellakatam ledu, sperm fertilise cheyyakatam ledu.

PCOS ki Yevari ki Tests Kavali?

  • Blood tests: LH, FSH, AMH, testosterone, prolactin, thyroid, fasting insulin + glucose
  • Pelvic ultrasound: ovarian morphology, antral follicle count (AFC)
  • Husband: semen analysis (both partner fertility check important)

PCOS Pregnancy Treatment Options — Step by Step

  1. Lifestyle change: Weight takkuva chesukonate (5% kuda), ovulation naturally start avvadam possible PCOS lo. Idi first step.
  2. Ovulation Induction: Letrozole or Clomiphene tablets — cycle day 2–5 nunchi 5 days. Timed intercourse or IUI tho combine chestaru. 3–4 cycles try chestamu.
  3. IUI (Intrauterine Insemination): Ovulation induction success lekapothe, IUI tho combine chestaru — success rate improve avutundi.
  4. IVF: IUI fail aite, IVF — PCOS patients ki antagonist protocol tho low-dose stimulation. OHSS risk tappistundi. Needleless IVF PCOS ki especially suitable.
  5. Laparoscopic Ovarian Drilling: Clomiphene-resistant PCOS ki — laparoscopy tho ovaries treat chestaru. 80% lo ovulation restore avutundi.
