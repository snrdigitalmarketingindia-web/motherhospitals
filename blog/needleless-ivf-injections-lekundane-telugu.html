Needleless IVF Ante Enti?

Saadharana IVF lo, 10–12 days rojulu daily FSH injections estaru — ovaries ni stimulate cheyyadaaniki. Ee injections abdomen lo inject chesukuntaru. Chala mandi patients ki injections bayam, pain unayi.

Needleless IVF lo, ee injections replace avutaayi — oral medications (tablets) and transdermal patches (skin patches) tho. Oka needle lekunda IVF possible avutundi.

Ela Pani Chestundi?

  • Oral gonadotropins (tablets) — ovaries stimulate chesatayi
  • Transdermal oestrogen/FSH patches — skin meedha paste chestaru, body loki slow release avutundi
  • Monitoring scans (ultrasound) regular ga chestaru — follicle growth check ki
  • Egg retrieval (OPU) same ga untundi — IV sedation tho, 20 minutes, patient same day home velthundi

Elanti Patients ki Needleless IVF Suitable?

  • Injections ki extreme fear (needle phobia) unte
  • PCOS patients — OHSS (ovarian hyperstimulation) risk takkuvaga undaaniki
  • First IVF cycle — gentle start prefer chesevaarike
  • Low ovarian reserve (low AMH) — high-dose injections extra eggs raavatayi kaadu, gentle protocol also same result isthundi

Needleless IVF Success Rate Saadharana IVF Same aa?

Selected patients lo — yes. Needleless IVF lo retrieved eggs takkuvaga untayi (average 4–8 vs 8–12 in conventional IVF). Kaani egg quality same ga untundi. PCOS patients lo OHSS risk avoid avutundi — adi important advantage.

High ovarian reserve patients lo (AMH 3+, PCOS) needleless IVF actually better — because OHSS risk tappistundi. Low ovarian reserve patients lo kuda consider cheyavachu — Dr. Prashanthi Reddy individually decide chestundi.

East Hyderabad lo Idi Only Mother Hospitals lo

Needleless IVF idi Jubilee Hills, Banjara Hills lo kuda rare. East Hyderabad — Uppal, Boduppal, Nagole, Ghatkesar, Medipally corridor lo sirf Mother Hospitals lo matrame available.
