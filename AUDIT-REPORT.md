# Mother Hospitals Website Audit Report

**Audit Date:** 2026-06-27  
**Audited by:** Claude Code (automated extraction + analysis)  
**Scope:** 546 HTML files (415 non-blog pages + 131 blog posts)

---

## Executive Summary

- The site has strong SEO fundamentals on all 29 key pages audited: every page has a title, meta description, canonical tag, schema markup, FAQ markup, and CTA — the baseline technical SEO is solid.
- The IVF/fertility cluster is heavily over-indexed (250+ pages on IVF/ICSI/IUI/location variants) while Pregnancy Care (17 pages), Advanced Gynae Surgery, and Wellness are severely under-represented.
- 51 service pages are completely missing — including every dedicated scan type (NT scan, anomaly scan, growth scan, 4D scan), all advanced fertility lab procedures (TESA/PESA, PGT-A, ERA test, assisted hatching, IMSI), all cosmetic/reconstructive gynae pages, and all wellness/screening pages (HPV vaccine, Pap smear, breast screening).
- Seven Telugu service pages and three Hindi hub pages are missing despite active demand from local-language audiences already on the site.
- Three clusters of near-duplicate pages dilute SEO authority: IVF explanation (3 pages), IVF cost (4 English + 2 location variants), and recurrent miscarriage (2 English + 4 location variants) — these need consolidation before further expansion.

## 5. Pages Requiring Consolidation

1. **IVF Explanation Cluster** — what-is-ivf-hyderabad.html, ivf-procedure-explained.html, ivf-treatment-step-by-step.html all rank for the same queries. Merge into one ~2,500-word definitive page, retaining the best content from all three.

2. **IVF Cost Cluster** — affordable-ivf-hyderabad.html, low-cost-ivf-packages-hyderabad.html, ivf-cost-hyderabad.html cannibalize each other. A single "IVF Cost Hyderabad 2026" page with a pricing table, EMI section, and package comparison is stronger.

3. **Recurrent Miscarriage Duplicate** — recurrent-miscarriage-treatment-hyderabad.html and recurrent-miscarriage-hyderabad.html. The main -treatment- URL should become the sole canonical; the other 301-redirected.

4. **Donor Egg vs Egg Donation** — donor-egg-ivf-hyderabad.html (for patients seeking donor eggs) and egg-donation-hyderabad.html (for donors) are legitimately different audiences. Add a clear patient/donor split above the fold on each, link between them, and differentiate meta titles clearly.

5. **Doctor Profile Duplication** — dr-prashanthi-reddy.html and dr-prashanthi-reddy-fertility-specialist-hyderabad.html. Merge into dr-prashanthi-reddy.html with expanded content (clinical background, publications, video, patient stories). 301 the second URL.

6. **Needleless / Injection-Free IVF** — Consolidate into needleless-ivf-hyderabad.html as the definitive page.
