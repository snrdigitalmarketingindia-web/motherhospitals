About Dr. Prashanthi Reddy

Dr. E. Prashanthi Reddy is the Founder and Clinical Director of Mother Hospitals & IVF Center, Boduppal, Hyderabad — one of East Hyderabad's most trusted fertility centres. With over 19 years of dedicated experience in reproductive medicine, she has helped more than 1,000 couples achieve parenthood through personalised IVF, ICSI, IUI, and advanced fertility treatments.

Trained in Germany at the prestigious Christian-Albrechts-Universität zu Kiel (Kiel University), Dr. Prashanthi brings international ART (Assisted Reproductive Technology) expertise to Hyderabad. She is known for her honest counselling, patient-specific protocols, and her ability to give hope to couples who have been told IVF is impossible — including those with very low AMH, severe male factor, and repeated IVF failures.

She personally leads every IVF cycle — from first consultation through egg retrieval, embryo culture, and transfer — ensuring continuity of care that larger hospital chains cannot offer. Her clinic is registered with the Telangana Medical Council (TGMC Reg: 50624) and fully certified under India's ART (Regulation) Act 2021.
