About Dr. E. Prashanthi Reddy

Dr. E. Prashanthi Reddy is one of Hyderabad's most experienced and compassionate fertility specialists and gynaecologists, with over 19 years of clinical practice in women's health, fertility treatments, obstetrics, and cosmetic gynaecology. She is the Founder & Medical Director of Mother Hospitals & IVF Center, Boduppal — and also leads Prashanthi Hospital, Choutuppal.

After completing her MBBS at Mamatha Medical College, Khammam, and her DGO (Diploma in Gynaecology & Obstetrics) at Gandhi Medical College, Hyderabad, Dr. Prashanthi pursued advanced training in Assisted Reproductive Technology at Kiel University, Germany (2021), and holds a Cosmetic Gynecology Certification (2024). She has personally overseen more than 5,000 IVF cycles and has helped over 10,000 families build their dreams.

Dr. Prashanthi is a strong advocate for accessible, patient-centred fertility care. She pioneered Needleless IVF at Mother Hospitals — a gentler stimulation protocol that reduces discomfort and medication costs without compromising outcomes. She is particularly renowned for managing complex cases including low AMH, PCOS, recurrent pregnancy loss, and male factor infertility.

Dr. Prashanthi's Treatment Philosophy

Personalised Protocols, Not Cookie-Cutter IVF

Every patient who walks into Mother Hospitals receives a tailored stimulation and treatment protocol based on their individual hormonal profile, ovarian reserve (AMH, AFC), age, and history. Dr. Prashanthi firmly believes that a one-size approach to IVF leads to suboptimal outcomes — personalisation is where results improve.

Minimal Stimulation & Needleless IVF

For patients with poor ovarian reserve or needle anxiety, Dr. Prashanthi offers minimal stimulation IVF and Needleless IVF using oral medications (Letrozole, Clomid) combined with low-dose FSH. This approach reduces cost by 30–40% and eliminates OHSS risk, while maintaining good blastocyst quality.

ERA Test & Endometrial Optimisation

For patients who have experienced repeated implantation failure, Dr. Prashanthi incorporates the ERA (Endometrial Receptivity Analysis) test to identify the precise window of implantation, often uncovering a 2–3 hour displacement that standard protocols miss. This has improved outcomes for her recurrent failure patients significantly.

Holistic Care & Emotional Support

Fertility treatment is emotionally demanding. Mother Hospitals provides dedicated counselling sessions, dietary guidance, and peer support groups led by patients who have successfully conceived. Dr. Prashanthi believes that a supported patient is a more resilient patient — and better outcomes follow from emotional wellbeing.
