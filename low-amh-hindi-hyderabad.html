ज़रूरी बात

Low AMH mein pregnancy ho sakti hai kya?

Haan — AMH kam hone par bhi pregnancy possible hai. AMH (Anti-Müllerian Hormone) ovarian reserve ka indicator hai — kitne eggs bache hain yeh batata hai. Low AMH matlab eggs ki quantity kam — lekin quality theek ho sakti hai. DuoStim, Mini-IVF, Mild stimulation protocols se AMH 0.3 hone par bhi eggs retrieve ho sakte hain. Call: 97059 93366.

हमारी Services

📉 AMH Kya Hota Hai?

AMH (Anti-Müllerian Hormone) ek blood test hai jo ovarian reserve measure karta hai. Normal: 1.0–4.0 ng/mL. Low: 0.3–1.0. Bahut low: 0.3 se kam. AMH kam matlab eggs kam hain — lekin ek bhi egg pregnancy ke liye kaafi hai!

🔄 DuoStim Protocol Kya Hai?

Ek menstrual cycle mein do baar eggs collect karte hain — follicular phase mein ek baar, luteal phase mein ek baar. Isse low AMH patients mein zyada eggs mil jaate hain. Mother Hospitals mein available.

🌿 Mini-IVF / Mild Stimulation

Kam dose ki medicines se gentle stimulation. Kam eggs milte hain lekin quality better hoti hai. High dose stimulation se low AMH mein mild approach often better results deti hai.

❄️ Embryo Accumulation Strategy

2–3 IVF cycles mein embryos collect karke freeze kar lete hain. Sab milake transfer karte hain. Ek cycle mein enough embryos na milne wale low AMH patients ke liye best approach.

🥚 Egg Freezing — Pehle Se Save Karein

AMH slowly decline hota hai — umar ke saath. 30–35 saal mein jab AMH theek ho tab eggs freeze kara lein — baad mein IVF ke liye use ho sakta hai.

🧬 Donor Egg IVF — Last Option

Khud ke eggs se IVF baar baar fail ho jaaye — tab donor egg IVF consider kar sakte hain. ART Act 2021 certified anonymous donor program Mother Hospitals mein available. 60–70% success rate.

अक्सर पूछे जाने वाले Questions

AMH 0.5 ho to IVF possible hai kya?
Haan. AMH 0.5 low hai — lekin impossible nahi. DuoStim ya mild stimulation protocol se eggs retrieve kar sakte hain. Dr. Prashanthi aapka AMH aur AFC dekh ke best protocol decide karti hain.
AMH naturally badh sakta hai kya?
Significantly nahi — AMH bache hue follicles ki count reflect karta hai jo increase nahi hoti. Lekin DHEA, CoQ10 supplements egg quality thoda improve kar sakte hain. Dr. Prashanthi aapke case ke hisaab se supplements recommend karti hain.
DuoStim kitna cost karta hai?
DuoStim standard IVF se thoda zyada hai — kyunki do retrieval procedures hote hain. Lekin zyada eggs milte hain — overall cost-per-embryo kam ho jaata hai. Exact cost ke liye 97059 93366 call karein.
Low AMH mein kitni baar IVF karna pad sakta hai?
Ek cycle mein kam eggs milte hain — isliye 2–3 cycles mein embryos accumulate karke transfer karte hain (embryo banking strategy). Ek baar mein enough embryos mil jaayein to single cycle kaafi hai. Dr. Prashanthi clearly batati hain.
Ek hospital ne bola "impossible" — kya sach mein ho nahi sakta?
Zaroor second opinion lein. "Impossible" bahut jaldi bolna doctor ki limitation ho sakti hai, aapki nahi. AMH 0.1 tak bhi kuch cases mein eggs milte hain. Mother Hospitals mein DuoStim, Mini-IVF try karne ke baad hi final decision lein.
