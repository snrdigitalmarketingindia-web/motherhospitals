Quick Answer

What happens at the first IVF consultation at Mother Hospitals?

Your first consultation at Mother Hospitals (30–45 minutes) covers: complete fertility history review, interpretation of any previous test results you bring, recommendation of baseline investigations if not done (AMH, Day 2 panel, AFC ultrasound, semen analysis), explanation of likely diagnosis and treatment options, personalised protocol discussion, and transparent cost breakdown. No high-pressure sales. No unnecessary tests. You leave with a clear action plan. Call 97059 93366 to book.

What We Offer

📋 What to Bring

Previous fertility reports (AMH, FSH, LH, semen analysis), ultrasound reports, discharge summaries from previous IVF cycles (if any), list of current medications, and any questions you have written down.

🔬 Investigations on Day 1

If you have not had recent tests, Dr. Prashanthi may request: AMH blood test (any day), Day 2 pelvic ultrasound (antral follicle count), thyroid (TSH), prolactin, and a semen analysis for your partner.

🩺 Physical Examination

Transvaginal ultrasound to assess uterine cavity, endometrial lining, ovarian appearance, and antral follicle count. This single scan gives Dr. Prashanthi the most important fertility information.

💬 Honest Diagnosis & Options

Dr. Prashanthi explains your diagnosis clearly — no jargon, no false promises. Treatment options presented from least invasive (IUI) to most (IVF, donor IVF) with realistic success rates for your specific profile.

💰 Transparent Cost Breakdown

All-inclusive IVF package price explained at the consultation — no surprises. Itemised cost breakdown covering stimulation, egg retrieval, ICSI, embryo culture, transfer, and luteal support.

❓ Questions to Ask Dr. Prashanthi

"What is my AMH and what does it mean?" "What is my IVF success rate estimate?" "What protocol would you use for me?" "How many cycles might I need?" "What is the cost, all-in?"

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to come on a specific day of my cycle for the first consultation?
No — the first consultation can happen any day. However, if Dr. Prashanthi wants to do a Day 2 AFC ultrasound and baseline blood tests, you may be asked to return on Day 2–3 of your next period. The initial history and discussion appointment can happen any day.
Should my husband/partner come to the first consultation?
Yes — strongly recommended. Male factor is involved in 40–50% of infertility cases. Bringing your partner means a semen analysis can be arranged immediately, and both of you understand the diagnosis and plan together from the beginning.
What if I have had IVF before at another clinic?
Bring all your previous cycle records — stimulation protocols used, egg numbers, fertilisation rates, embryo quality, transfer details, and outcome. Dr. Prashanthi reviews previous cycles to identify what to do differently. A second opinion consultation at Mother Hospitals is free.
How soon can I start IVF after the first consultation?
If investigations are already done and results are normal, IVF can often start in the same or next menstrual cycle — sometimes within 2–4 weeks of the first consultation. If additional tests are needed, there may be a 4–8 week preparation period.
Is the IVF consultation free at Mother Hospitals?
Call 97059 93366 for current consultation fees. Second opinions for patients who have had previous IVF cycles elsewhere are always discussed openly. Consultation fees are typically adjusted against the IVF treatment cost.
