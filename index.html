Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about IVF, fertility treatments and Mother Hospitals & IVF Center.

Dr. E. Prashanthi Reddy — MBBS, DGO, PG Diploma in ART (Kiel University, Germany) — is the Founder & Medical Director of Mother Hospitals & IVF Center, Boduppal. With 19+ years of experience in IVF, fertility treatments, obstetrics and gynaecology, she is widely regarded as one of the best fertility specialists in East Hyderabad.

What is Needleless IVF and is it available in Hyderabad?+

Needleless IVF is a pain-free IVF protocol that avoids conventional injection-based stimulation. It uses oral medications and minimal stimulation, making it ideal for patients who fear injections. Mother Hospitals & IVF Center is among the very few centres in Hyderabad offering Needleless IVF.

Can IVF work with Low AMH levels?+

Yes. Low AMH does not rule out IVF success. At Mother Hospitals, we use specialised low AMH protocols, Natural Cycle IVF and Mini IVF to maximise egg retrieval even with diminished ovarian reserve. Dr. Prashanthi Reddy has extensive experience managing poor ovarian responders.

What should I do if my IVF has failed multiple times?+

Recurrent IVF failure has specific causes that can be identified and treated. Mother Hospitals offers ERA (Endometrial Receptivity Assay), Laser Assisted Hatching, advanced embryo culture and personalised protocol review for patients who have experienced repeated IVF failures.

Does Mother Hospitals treat NRI patients?+

Yes. Mother Hospitals & IVF Center welcomes patients from the USA, UK, Gulf, Middle East and across India. We offer remote initial consultations, pre-arrival treatment planning, and dedicated NRI care coordination so your visit to Hyderabad is smooth and efficient.

Is Mother Hospitals ART Act 2021 certified?+

Yes. Mother Hospitals & IVF Center is fully certified under the ART (Assisted Reproductive Technology) Regulation Act 2021 — the government regulatory framework for IVF clinics in India. This ensures compliance with the highest safety, ethical and quality standards.
