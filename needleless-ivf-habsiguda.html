FAQs — Habsiguda Patients

What is Needleless IVF?
Needleless IVF replaces the 10–14 daily FSH self-injection routine of standard IVF with an alternative stimulation protocol (oral tablets, nasal sprays, or low-frequency clinic-administered injections). The egg retrieval, ICSI, and embryo transfer remain the same.
Is Needleless IVF as effective as standard IVF?
For suitable candidates — typically women with normal to good ovarian reserve — Needleless IVF achieves comparable results to standard IVF. Dr. Prashanthi Reddy assesses your AMH and AFC to determine whether you are a good candidate.
