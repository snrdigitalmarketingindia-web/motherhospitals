What We Offer — Serving Habsiguda Patients

🔬 What is ICSI?

Single sperm selected and injected directly into the egg under high-power microscope. 70–75% fertilisation rate even with very poor sperm. The standard for male factor infertility worldwide.

👨 ICSI for All Male Factor

Low count, poor motility, abnormal morphology, high DNA fragmentation — ICSI overcomes the barrier of poor sperm quality, achieving fertilisation that natural or standard IVF cannot.

🏥 TESA + ICSI for Azoospermia

Zero sperm in ejaculate? TESA retrieves sperm from the testis surgically — same day as egg retrieval — used immediately for ICSI.

💉 ICSI After Failed IVF Fertilisation

If previous standard IVF had poor or zero fertilisation, ICSI in the next cycle almost always achieves fertilisation.

🧬 ICSI + PGT-A Embryo Testing

ICSI embryos biopsied for chromosomal testing — only chromosomally normal embryos transferred, reducing miscarriage risk significantly.

FAQs — Habsiguda Patients

Does stress from IT work near Habsiguda affect sperm quality?
Yes — prolonged work stress, sedentary lifestyle, irregular sleep, and heat exposure from laptops can all reduce sperm count, motility, and morphology. Many IT professionals from Habsiguda and DRDO Colony present with sperm quality issues. The good news: sperm renews every 72 days, so lifestyle changes + ICSI can achieve very good results.
What sperm count is needed for ICSI near Habsiguda?
ICSI works with even a very small number of sperm — technically just a few sperm per egg. There is no minimum count threshold for ICSI. Even with zero sperm in the ejaculate (azoospermia), TESA can retrieve sperm directly from the testis for ICSI.
Is ICSI safe near Habsiguda?
ICSI has been performed for 30+ years with tens of millions of births worldwide. Birth defect rates, childhood development, and health outcomes are comparable to natural conception. The procedure is very safe and is the global standard for male infertility.
How long does ICSI take near Habsiguda?
ICSI is performed in the embryology lab on the day of egg retrieval — about 1–2 hours for the fertilisation procedure. The IVF stimulation phase leading up to egg retrieval takes 10–14 days of monitoring. Embryo transfer is 3–5 days after egg retrieval (Day 3 or blastocyst Day 5).
