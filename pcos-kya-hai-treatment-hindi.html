PCOS kya hota hai? Pregnancy mein kya dikkat hoti hai?

PCOS (Polycystic Ovary Syndrome) — ovaries mein chhote chhote cysts ban jaate hain, jis se hormones ka balance bigad jaata hai. Ovulation irregular ho jaata hai ya aata hi nahi. हमारी Services

🦋 PCOS Kya Hota Hai?

Ovaries mein bahut saare chhote follicles bante hain — lekin koi bhi mature nahi hota, ovulation nahi aata. Androgen (male hormone) levels high ho jaate hain. 10 mein se 1 woman ko PCOS hota hai — bahut common condition.

⚠️ PCOS Ke Symptoms Kya Hain?

Periods irregular (2–3 mahine mein ek baar ya bilkul nahi). Weight gain, especially belly fat. Facial hair, acne. Hair fall. Ovulation na hone ki wajah se pregnancy mein dikkat. Ultrasound mein ovaries mein multiple cysts dikhna.

💊 PCOS Treatment — Step by Step

1) Lifestyle changes (diet, exercise) — sabse pehla step. 2) Metformin + ovulation tablets (letrozole/clomiphene). 3) Ovulation aa jaaye to IUI try kar sakte hain. 4) Sab fail ho to IVF. Bahut si women step 2–3 mein hi pregnant ho jaati hain.

🥗 Diet Aur Weight Kyu Zaroori Hai?

PCOS mein insulin resistance common hai — low glycemic diet se help milti hai. White rice, maida kam karein. Vegetables, protein badhayein. Roz 30 minute walk se ovulation improve hota hai. 5% weight loss bhi ovulation trigger kar sakta hai.

📊 PCOS Mein Success Rate Kitna?

Ovulation induction + IUI success rate: 15–25% per cycle. IVF mein PCOS patients ka result actually better hota hai — kyunki eggs zyada hote hain. OHSS risk manage karte hain — safe treatment.

🔬 PCOS Diagnosis Kaise?

Blood tests: LH, FSH, testosterone, AMH, fasting insulin, thyroid. Ultrasound: ovaries mein 12+ follicles per ovary (AFC), ovary size badi hogi. In tests se clearly diagnose ho jaata hai. Pehli visit mein hi complete workup ho sakta hai.

अक्सर पूछे जाने वाले Questions

Kya PCOS mein naturally pregnant ho sakte hain?
Haan — treatment se. Weight loss + lifestyle se ovulation naturally shuru ho sakta hai. Tablets se ovulation trigger karke IVF ke bina pregnant ho sakte hain. Sabhi options try karne ke baad hi IVF discuss karte hain.
PCOS mein periods irregular hain — kya karein?
Dr. Prashanthi ke paas aayein. Blood tests + ultrasound se PCOS confirm ho jayega. Lifestyle changes + metformin se periods regular ho sakte hain. Pregnancy try kar rahe hain to ovulation induction + IUI ya IVF plan discuss hoga.
Thyroid + PCOS dono ho to kya problem hogi?
Dono common combination hai — phir bhi treat ho sakta hai. Pehle thyroid control karna zaroori hai (TSH 2.5 se kam pregnancy ke liye). Phir PCOS treatment. Dr. Prashanthi dono simultaneously manage karti hain.
PCOS mein IVF karte waqt OHSS ka darr rehta hai?
PCOS patients mein OHSS (Ovarian Hyperstimulation Syndrome) ka risk thoda zyada hota hai — kyunki eggs zyada hote hain. Lekin Mother Hospitals mein low-dose protocol + freeze-all strategy use karte hain — OHSS risk minimize ho jaata hai.
Weight kam karne se PCOS theek ho jaayega kya?
Theek nahi — lekin bahut improve ho jaata hai. 5–10% weight loss se ovulation wapas aa sakta hai, periods regular ho sakte hain, pregnancy chance better ho jaata hai. PCOS long-term condition hai — manage kar sakte hain.
