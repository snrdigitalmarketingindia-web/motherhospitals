Which Package Is Right for You?

Choosing the Right IVF Package

Standard IVF (₹99,000) — Best For:

First-time IVF patients under 35 with unexplained infertility, tubal factor, mild PCOS, or mild male factor. Normal to good AMH and ovarian reserve. Optimistic about a single-cycle approach.

IVF+ICSI (₹1,20,000) — Best For:

Couples with moderate to severe male factor infertility (low motility, poor morphology, low count). Also recommended for previous fertilisation failures or when using surgically retrieved sperm.

3-Cycle Plan (₹2,50,000) — Best For:

Women over 35, low AMH patients, recurrent implantation failure, or anyone who wants maximum cumulative chances with predictable total cost. Most cost-effective per-cycle option.

IVF + 2 FET Plan (₹1,50,000) — Best For:

PCOS patients (Freeze-All avoids OHSS). Also ideal for patients who want to bank embryos from one stimulation for multiple transfer attempts over 2–3 years without repeat egg retrievals.

FAQ

IVF Package — Frequently Asked Questions

What are the IVF package prices in Hyderabad in 2026?
At Mother Hospitals, IVF packages start at ₹99,000 all-inclusive for a standard IVF cycle. IVF+ICSI is ₹1,20,000 all-inclusive. Frozen embryo transfer (FET) is ₹30,000 per cycle. Multi-cycle packages are available from ₹1,80,000 for 2 cycles and ₹2,50,000 for 3 cycles.
What does the ₹99,000 IVF package include?
Our ₹99,000 all-inclusive package covers consultation, all medications, monitoring scans, egg retrieval with anaesthesia, embryo culture to blastocyst stage, embryo transfer, and post-transfer consultation. No separate medication bills or scan charges.
Is it worth getting a multi-cycle IVF package?
Yes, if you are above 35 or have a diagnosis that may require more than one cycle. A 3-cycle package saves significantly compared to paying cycle by cycle, and the cumulative success rate over 3 cycles can reach 80%+ for women under 38.
Are IVF medication costs included in the package?
Yes. All stimulation medications are included in our ₹99,000 all-inclusive IVF package. You will not receive a separate pharmacy bill for stimulation medications — they are fully covered.
Is there a package for Needleless IVF?
Needleless IVF is available within our standard ₹99,000 all-inclusive package at no extra cost. It replaces injectable stimulation with oral tablets and transdermal patches for eligible patients — same price, fewer injections.
What if I have frozen embryos from a previous cycle elsewhere?
If you have frozen embryos from a previous clinic, you can use our frozen embryo transfer (FET) service at ₹30,000. We will require detailed lab documentation from your previous clinic. Contact us to discuss the logistics of your specific case.
Can I pay for the IVF package in EMI?
Yes. All our IVF packages are available on EMI. The 3-cycle plan is available from approximately ₹9,000/month on a 24-month plan. Ask our financial counsellor about no-cost EMI options on your credit card.
