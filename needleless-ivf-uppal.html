FAQs — Uppal Patients

What is Needleless IVF and how does it work?
Needleless IVF replaces the 10–14 daily FSH injection routine with an alternative stimulation protocol using oral tablets, nasal sprays, or low-frequency clinic injections. Egg retrieval, ICSI, and embryo transfer remain the same as standard IVF.
Is Needleless IVF suitable for me near Uppal?
Needleless IVF is suitable for women with needle anxiety, normal-to-good ovarian reserve (AMH above 1 ng/mL), and those who want a less physically demanding IVF cycle. Dr. Prashanthi assesses your AMH and AFC to confirm suitability.
Is Needleless IVF as effective as standard IVF?
For suitable candidates, success rates are comparable to standard IVF. Some women even respond better due to reduced stress. Dr. Prashanthi Reddy determines the right protocol for your specific profile.
