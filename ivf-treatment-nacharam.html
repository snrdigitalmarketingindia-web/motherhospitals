Our Services

🧬 IVF — What to Expect

Stimulation (10–14 days injections/tablets) → monitoring scans → egg retrieval (sedation) → ICSI fertilisation → Day 5 blastocyst culture → embryo transfer → pregnancy test Day 14. All under one roof at Boduppal.

📊 Success Rates

Under 35: 55–65% per cycle. 35–38: 40–50%. 38–40: 30–40%. Over 40 own eggs: 20–30%. Donor egg IVF 60–70%. Success depends on age, diagnosis, and protocol — Dr. Prashanthi advises honestly.

🌿 Needleless IVF Available

Fear injections? Mother Hospitals offers Needleless IVF — tablet-based stimulation, no daily injections. Ideal for mild responders and needle-phobic patients. Same success rates for suitable candidates.

❄️ Freeze All Strategy

All embryos frozen for transfer next cycle when needed — better outcomes, zero OHSS risk. Frozen embryo transfer (FET) success matches fresh transfer. Remaining embryos stored for future attempts.

🧪 PGT-A Genetic Testing

Embryo biopsied at Day 5, all 24 chromosomes tested. Only genetically normal embryo transferred. Reduces miscarriage risk from 30%+ to under 5%. Recommended for age 38+, recurrent failure, recurrent miscarriage.
