What makes Needleless IVF different from standard IVF?

Standard IVF uses daily FSH/hMG injections (8–12 days of self-administered injections) for ovarian stimulation. Needleless IVF uses oral medications — Letrozole and Clomiphene — combined with very low-dose FSH injections, dramatically reducing the total injection count. Benefits: 30–40% lower medication cost, near-zero OHSS risk, and suitable for poor responders (low AMH), older patients, and anyone with needle anxiety. Dr. Prashanthi is one of the few Hyderabad specialists who offers this as a primary protocol.
