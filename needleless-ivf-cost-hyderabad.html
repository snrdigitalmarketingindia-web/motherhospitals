What's Included & Excluded

We believe in complete transparency. Here's exactly what our all-inclusive Needleless IVF package covers — and what may be charged separately if clinically needed.

✅ Included in the Package

  • Initial fertility consultation with Dr. E. Prashanthi Reddy
  • Baseline blood tests (AMH, FSH, LH, E2)
  • Baseline antral follicle count ultrasound
  • All oral stimulation medications (letrozole / clomiphene)
  • Monitoring ultrasound scans during stimulation
  • Trigger (nasal spray / oral)
  • Anaesthesia for egg retrieval (OPU)
  • ICSI fertilisation in our laboratory
  • Embryo culture to Day 3 or Day 5 (blastocyst)
  • Fresh embryo transfer

ℹ️ May Attract Additional Cost

  • PGT-A genetic embryo testing (if recommended)
  • Embryo freezing / vitrification (if freeze-all chosen)
  • Frozen embryo transfer (FET) in a subsequent cycle
  • ERA (Endometrial Receptivity Array) test
  • Sperm DNA fragmentation testing
  • Donor sperm (if required)
  • Nasal hMG spray if added to protocol (some cases)
  • Repeat consultation if cycle cancelled

Our counsellor will always discuss and get your approval before any additional charges are incurred.

Frequently Asked Questions

Needleless IVF Cost — FAQs

How much does Needleless IVF cost in Hyderabad? +

Needleless IVF at Mother Hospitals starts from all-inclusive — covering consultations, scans, egg retrieval, ICSI, embryo culture, and embryo transfer. Total cost with oral medications is typically ₹1,04,000–₹1,14,000 per cycle.

Is Needleless IVF cheaper than conventional IVF? +

Overall, yes — in many cases. While the base procedure cost is similar, oral stimulation medications cost significantly less than injectable gonadotropins (saving ₹10,000–₹35,000 on medication alone). This makes the total cycle cost competitive or lower than conventional IVF.

Does insurance cover Needleless IVF in India? +

Most standard Indian health insurance plans do not cover IVF. Some employer group policies provide partial coverage. We recommend checking your policy. We offer EMI plans to make treatment accessible regardless of insurance status.

Is EMI available for Needleless IVF at Mother Hospitals? +

Yes. We offer EMI options through partner finance providers. 0% or low-interest plans are available. Speak to our counsellor at your first consultation for current EMI schemes and eligibility criteria.

What add-ons increase the cost significantly? +

PGT-A genetic testing (₹30,000–₹60,000) and embryo freezing + FET (₹40,000–₹65,000 combined) are the main add-ons that increase cost. These are only recommended when clinically indicated — never added routinely.

What if my first Needleless IVF cycle fails — do I pay full price again? +

If embryos were frozen from the first cycle, a Frozen Embryo Transfer (FET) costs significantly less (₹25,000–₹40,000) than a full fresh cycle. Multi-cycle packages also offer savings. Our counsellor will discuss the most cost-effective path at your review consultation.

Are there any hidden charges at Mother Hospitals? +

No. We provide a full cost breakdown before treatment starts. Any additional investigation or procedure is discussed with you first and no charge is added without your knowledge and consent.

Can I get Needleless IVF on a tight budget? +

Yes. Mother Hospitals offers competitive pricing, EMI options, and a transparent no-hidden-charges policy. Our counsellors will help design the most effective yet affordable plan for your specific situation. Call 97059 93366 to discuss.
