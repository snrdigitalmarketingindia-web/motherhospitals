## Executive Summary

Mother Hospitals has built a technically impressive, large-scale fertility website (552 pages) with strong foundational SEO infrastructure — schema markup on 413+ pages, trust bar signals on every page, and good FAQ coverage. However, the site has significant gaps in **proven clinical outcomes**, **third-party credibility signals**, **patient journey content**, and **topical cluster depth** that prevent it from dominating Hyderabad fertility searches against Nova IVF, Oasis, and Cloudnine.

**Overall Site Score: 67/100**
