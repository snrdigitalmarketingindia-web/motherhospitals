Who Needs High-Risk Pregnancy Care?

The following conditions require specialised pregnancy monitoring. Mother Hospitals in Boduppal manages all of these:

Twin / multiple pregnancy
IVF-conceived pregnancy
Diabetes in pregnancy (GDM)
Hypertension / pre-eclampsia
Advanced maternal age (35+)
Previous C-section (VBAC evaluation)
Placenta previa / low-lying placenta
Thyroid disorders in pregnancy
Preterm labour risk
Recurrent pregnancy loss history
Anaemia in pregnancy
Foetal growth restriction (FGR)

Frequently Asked Questions — Pregnancy Care, Boduppal

Is Mother Hospitals a maternity hospital in Boduppal?
Yes. Mother Hospitals & IVF Center is located in Aakruthi Township, Boduppal, Hyderabad – 500092. It provides comprehensive pregnancy care including antenatal check-ups, TIFFA scans, GDM screening, high-risk pregnancy monitoring, normal delivery support, and C-section (LSCS) facilities.
What pregnancy care services are available in Boduppal at Mother Hospitals?
Monthly and fortnightly antenatal check-ups, anomaly scan / TIFFA scan (18–20 weeks), gestational diabetes (GDM) screening, high-risk pregnancy monitoring, normal vaginal delivery, C-section (LSCS), post-natal care, IVF pregnancy monitoring, twin pregnancy management, and pre-eclampsia & hypertension management.
Can I get IVF and continue my pregnancy care at the same hospital?
Yes. Mother Hospitals is both an IVF centre and a maternity hospital. Couples who conceive through IVF continue their entire pregnancy under the same team — Dr. E. Prashanthi Reddy manages both the IVF cycle and the subsequent pregnancy. This continuity is especially important for IVF pregnancies, which require closer monitoring.
Is high-risk pregnancy managed at Mother Hospitals Boduppal?
Yes. Specialised high-risk care is available for: twin pregnancies, diabetes in pregnancy (GDM), pre-eclampsia, hypertension, IVF pregnancies, advanced maternal age (35+), previous C-section, placenta previa, thyroid disorders, and preterm labour risk.

Are normal delivery and C-section facilities available in Boduppal?
