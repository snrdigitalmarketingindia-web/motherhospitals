ज़रूरी बात

IUI (Intrauterine Insemination) — husband ke sperm ko lab mein wash karke seedha uterus mein dala jaata hai jab ovulation ho raha ho. Success rate: 20–30% per medicated cycle. IVF se bahut sasta. हमारी Services

💉 IUI Kaise Hota Hai?

Din 2 — baseline ultrasound. Din 3–7 — ovulation induction tablets (letrozole ya clomiphene). Din 10–12 — follicle monitoring scan. Follicle ready hone par HCG trigger injection. 36 ghante baad — IUI procedure (15 minute, no anaesthesia). Din 14 — pregnancy blood test.

🎯 IUI Kiske Liye Sahi Hai?

1–2 saal se try kar rahe hain bina kisi badi problem ke. Mild male factor (count 10M+). PCOS — ovulation induction ke saath. Cervical mucus problem. Fallopian tubes khuli hain. Unexplained infertility — sab normal lekin pregnancy nahi.

📊 Success Rate — Kitni Umeed?

Natural IUI: 10–15%. Medicated IUI: 20–30%. Umar kam ho aur problem mild ho to better result. 3 cycles try karte hain — agar kaam na kare to IVF discuss karte hain. Dr. Prashanthi har cycle ke baad honestly batati hain agla kadam kya ho.

💰 IUI Cost — IVF Se Bahut Kam

IUI mein egg retrieval nahi, embryology fees nahi, medicines bhi kam. IVF ke muqable mein fraction cost mein hota hai. Agar 3 cycles mein success mili to IVF ki zaroorat hi nahi — paise aur time dono bache.

🔄 3 IUI Fail Ho Jaaye To?

3 cycles ke baad review karte hain — koi naya finding? HSG (tube patency)? Semen analysis repeat? Agar koi naya reason nahi mila to IVF recommend. Dr. Prashanthi zyaada IUI karwake time waste nahi karti jab IVF clearly better option ho.

अक्सर पूछे जाने वाले Questions

IUI mein dard hota hai kya?
Nahi — IUI bilkul mild procedure hai. Catheter cervix se guzarte waqt period jaise thodi cramping feel hoti hai, 10–15 minute. Anaesthesia ki zaroorat nahi. Procedure ke baad seedha ghar ya kaam par ja sakte hain. Zyaadatar women ke liye bilkul comfortable hota hai.
Malkajgiri se IUI ke liye kitne chakkar laane padte hain?
Ek cycle mein: Day 2–3 baseline scan (1 visit) → Day 10–12 monitoring scan (1 visit) → IUI day (1 visit). Total 3 visits, 2 hafte mein. Har visit 20–30 minute clinic mein. Malkajgiri se 20 minute — teen visits manage karna bahut aasaan hai.
PCOS mein IUI kaam karta hai kya?
Haan — bahut well kaam karta hai jab ovulation induction (letrozole) ke saath combine kiya jaaye. PCOS mein ovulation nahi aata — tablets se ek-do follicles develop hote hain, phir IUI. Success rate 20–30% per cycle PCOS patients mein. 3 cycles try karte hain.
Kya husband ko har visit par aana padega?
Sirf IUI day par aana chahiye husband ko — fresh semen sample dene ke liye (private room available). Baki monitoring visits mein wife akele aa sakti hain. IUI day par 1–2 ghante lagte hain dono ke liye total.
IUI ya IVF — mujhe kaise pata chalega kaunsa sahi hai?
Agar naali khuli hai, umar 37 se kam hai, count reasonable hai — IUI pehle try karte hain. Agar naali band hai, severe male factor hai, umar 38+ hai, ya 3 IUI fail ho chuke hain — IVF better. Dr. Prashanthi first consultation mein hi clearly guide karti hain.
