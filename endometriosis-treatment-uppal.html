What We Offer — Serving Uppal Patients

🔍 Endometriosis Diagnosis

Ultrasound for chocolate cysts (endometriomas), CA-125 blood marker, MRI for deep endometriosis. Laparoscopy remains the gold standard for definitive diagnosis.

💊 Medical Management

GnRH agonists (Lupron), dienogest, combined OCP, progesterone-only therapy — hormonal suppression to reduce pain and prepare for fertility treatment.

🏥 Fertility-Preserving Surgery

Laparoscopic endometrioma cystectomy — removing the chocolate cyst while preserving surrounding ovarian tissue. Coordinated with your fertility plan.

🧊 Egg Freezing Before Surgery

Recommended before cystectomy — surgery can reduce AMH. Banking eggs first protects your reserve before any operation.

🌱 IVF for Endometriosis

GnRH agonist pre-treatment (3–6 months) then IVF — the “long protocol” significantly improves implantation in endometriosis patients.

🩺 Pain Management

Dysmenorrhoea (painful periods), dyspareunia (pain with intercourse), chronic pelvic pain — medical and procedural management alongside fertility care.

FAQs — Uppal Patients

What are signs of endometriosis near Uppal?
Severely painful periods (much worse than most women), chronic pelvic pain, pain during intercourse, heavy periods, pain during bowel movements, lower back pain, and difficulty conceiving despite regular unprotected intercourse. Some women have silent endometriosis — no symptoms but found during infertility investigation.
Does endometriosis cause infertility near Uppal?
Yes — endometriosis is found in 20–30% of women with infertility. It impairs fertility through: distorted anatomy (adhesions), toxic peritoneal environment affecting egg quality, chocolate cysts reducing ovarian reserve, and impaired implantation. However, many endometriosis women conceive with the right treatment.
Should I have surgery or IVF for endometriosis near Uppal?
Depends on stage and age: Stage 1–2 (mild) — go straight to IVF. Stage 3–4 (large cysts, adhesions) — may benefit from surgery first. Large chocolate cysts — freeze eggs, then surgery. Dr. Prashanthi plans the sequence based on your age, AMH, and cyst size.
Will endometriosis surgery affect my AMH near Uppal?
Yes — cystectomy (chocolate cyst surgery) can inadvertently remove healthy ovarian tissue alongside the cyst, reducing AMH. This is why Dr. Prashanthi often recommends egg freezing before surgery to bank eggs at their current quality.
