📉 Low Sperm Count Kyun Hota Hai?

Varicocele (scrotum ki nason mein swelling) — sabse common, treat ho sakta hai. Hormonal problem (testosterone kam, FSH zyada). Infection history. Lifestyle — smoking, alcohol, heat, obesity. Genetics (Y-chromosome microdeletion). Karan pata karne par hi sahi treatment hoga.

🧬 ICSI — Bahut Kam Sperm Mein Bhi Kaam Karta Hai

Ek healthy sperm directly egg mein inject kiya jaata hai. Count 1 lakh ya usse bhi kam ho — ICSI kaam karta hai. Fertilisation rate 70–80%. Mother Hospitals mein zyaadatar IVF cycles mein ICSI standard hai.

⚕️ TESA — Zero Sperm Ke Liye

Semen mein sperm nahi (azoospermia) — TESA mein testis se directly sperm nikalte hain. Local anaesthesia mein 20–30 minute ka procedure. 30–50% non-obstructive azoospermia mein sperm milta hai. Same din ICSI kiya jaata hai.

🧪 Sperm DNA Fragmentation Test

Count normal hai lekin IVF fail ho raha hai ya miscarriage aa raha hai? Sperm DNA fragmentation test karwayein. High fragmentation fertilisation aur embryo quality ko affect karta hai. Antioxidants + lifestyle se improve ho sakta hai. Ek common missed diagnosis.

💊 Medical Treatment Options

Varicocele repair (surgery ya interventional radiology). Hormonal treatment — clomiphene, FSH. Antioxidants — CoQ10, zinc, selenium, Vitamin C. Lifestyle — smoking band, weight kam, heat avoid. 3 mahine treat karke retest. Tab decide IVF ki zaroorat hai ya nahi.

अक्सर पूछे जाने वाले Questions

Semen analysis kahan se karayen Secunderabad ke paas?
Mother Hospitals Boduppal mein same-day semen analysis available hai — 2–4 ghante mein result. Private collection room. Secunderabad se 35 minute. Ya Secunderabad mein kisi bhi lab se karayen aur report leke aayein — Dr. Prashanthi review karengi.
Husband ka sperm count 5 million hai — kya IUI possible hai?
5 million total count se IUI ke liye washed motile count kaafi kam reh sakta hai. IUI ke liye 5–10 million washed motile chahiye. Is case mein ICSI IVF better aur more reliable option hai. Semen analysis + wash test se confirm hoga.
Azoospermia ka matlab kya hai — kya pregnancy ho sakti hai?
Azoospermia matlab semen mein sperm nahi. Do types: obstructive (nali blocked) — TESA mein almost 100% sperm milta hai. Non-obstructive (production nahi) — 30–50% cases mein sperm milta hai. Jo cases mein TESA se bhi nahi milta, wahan donor sperm IVF option hai.
Male infertility treatment mein husband ko kitni baar aana padega?
Semen analysis: ek baar. Consultation: ek baar dono saath. IVF cycle mein — husband egg retrieval day aur semen analysis ke liye aata hai. Baki monitoring visits mein sirf wife aati hai. Kul mein husband ke liye 2–3 visits sufficient hain.
Kya male infertility treatment ka koi side effect hota hai?
ICSI: koi direct side effect nahi husband ke liye — sirf sample dena hota hai. TESA: local anaesthesia mein kiya jaata hai, 1–2 din mild dard, painkillers se thik. Antioxidants: safe, no significant side effects. Varicocele surgery: 2–3 din rest, recovery smooth.
