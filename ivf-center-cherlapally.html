Which IVF center is closest to Cherlapally?
Mother Hospitals & IVF Center in Boduppal is one of the nearest dedicated IVF clinics for Cherlapally patients — approximately 11 km, 20–25 minutes by road via ECIL X Roads or Uppal Road. It has a full in-house embryology lab.

What is the OPD schedule?
OPD is open every day from Monday to Sunday, 10:30 AM to 1:30 PM. Appointments are recommended. Call 97059 93366 or WhatsApp 90520 74999 to book.
Can I consult online before visiting in person?
Yes, WhatsApp-based initial consultations are available for patients who want to discuss their case before travelling. Send your reports on WhatsApp (90520 74999) and our team will guide you on the next steps.
Is Mother Hospitals certified for IVF?
Yes. Mother Hospitals & IVF Center is certified under the ART (Regulation) Act 2021 and registered with TGMC (Reg: 50624). It complies with all regulatory requirements for assisted reproductive technology in India.
