हमारी Services

🧬 IVF Kya Hota Hai?

Stimulation — tablet/injection se ovaries ko eggs banane ke liye trigger kiya jaata hai (10–14 din). Egg retrieval — sedation mein eggs nikale jaate hain (20 min). Lab mein sperm se ICSI fertilisation. Day 5 tak embryo culture. Best embryo uterus mein transfer. 14 din baad pregnancy test.

📊 IVF Success Rate Kitna Hai?

35 saal se kam: 55–65% per cycle. 35–38: 40–50%. 38–40: 30–40%. Donor egg IVF: 60–70%. Success rate age, AMH, aur diagnosis pe depend karta hai. Dr. Prashanthi honest expectation first consultation mein hi deti hain — koi false promise nahi.

🌿 Needleless IVF — Injection Nahi

Tablet-based stimulation — roz injection nahi. Same lab, same doctor, same results suitable patients ke liye. Defence aur government families ke liye jo daily injection routine nahi chahte — bahut popular option.

🧪 PGT-A — Genetic Testing

Embryo ke sare 24 chromosomes test karte hain Day 5 par. Sirf chromosomally normal embryo transfer hota hai. Miscarriage rate 30%+ se ghar ke 5% se bhi kam aa jaata hai. Age 38+ aur repeated failure ke liye strongly recommended.

❄️ Frozen Embryo Transfer

Extra embryos freeze karke rakhte hain. FET (Frozen Embryo Transfer) fresh IVF se bahut sasta hota hai. Success rate same ya better. Ek retrieval cycle mein kaafi embryos mile to kai transfer attempts possible.

अक्सर पूछे जाने वाले Questions

Secunderabad se Mother Hospitals kitni door hai?
~22 km via Malkajgiri aur Uppal — approximately 35 minute by car. Metro option: Secunderabad se Uppal station (blue line), phir 5-minute auto. Monitoring visits ke liye (jo sabse zyada hoti hain) sirf 20–30 minute clinic mein lagte hain.
IVF shuru karne ke liye pehla kadam kya hai?
Fertility assessment: AMH blood test + antral follicle count scan (Day 2–3) + semen analysis. Sab ek din mein. Results ke baad Dr. Prashanthi protocol plan karti hain. Call 97059 93366 — period shuru hone par ya kisi bhi din appointment lein.
IVF mein kitne chakkar clinic ke laane padte hain?
Ek IVF cycle mein: 3–4 monitoring scans (10–14 din mein), egg retrieval day, embryo transfer day, pregnancy test. Total 6–8 visits, 3–4 hafte mein. Har monitoring visit 20–30 minute — Secunderabad se manage karna bilkul possible hai.
Kya Dr. Prashanthi khud consult karti hain ya junior doctors hote hain?
Dr. Prashanthi personally har consultation, egg retrieval, embryo transfer, aur follow-up karti hain. Kisi junior doctor ke haath mein case nahi jaata. Yahi wajah hai ki Secunderabad jaisi door jagah ke patients bhi yahan aate hain.
