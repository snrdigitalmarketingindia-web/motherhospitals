FAQs — Uppal Patients

What is the best age to freeze eggs near Uppal?
The best age is in your late 20s to early 30s — when egg quality and quantity are highest. Freezing at 28–32 gives significantly better results than freezing at 37+. The earlier, the better for egg quality.
How many eggs should I freeze near Uppal?
10–15 mature eggs generally give a good cumulative success rate for one baby. Younger women with good AMH often get this in 1 retrieval. Women with lower AMH may need 2–3 cycles. Dr. Prashanthi advises based on your age and AMH.
How long can frozen eggs be stored?
Under Indian ART Act 2021 regulations, eggs can be stored for up to 5 years (extendable). Storage is maintained in liquid nitrogen at −196°C — stability is excellent for many years.
What is the success rate of frozen eggs from near Uppal?
Vitrified eggs have a survival rate above 90% on thawing at Mother Hospitals. Fertilisation rates and pregnancy rates are comparable to fresh egg IVF for women who froze eggs before age 35.
