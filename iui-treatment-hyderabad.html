What is IUI?

Understanding IUI Treatment

IUI (Intrauterine Insemination) involves placing washed and concentrated sperm directly into the uterus around the time of ovulation. It increases the number of sperm that reach the fallopian tubes and improves chances of fertilisation.

Who is IUI For?

IUI is recommended for unexplained infertility, mild male factor infertility (low sperm count or motility), ovulation disorders (PCOS), cervical mucus issues, and couples where IVF is not yet needed. IUI requires at least 5 million total motile sperm after semen washing — below this, ICSI is more appropriate.

IUI Success Rate

IUI success rates are typically 10–20% per cycle. Success improves with multiple cycles. Most doctors recommend 3–6 IUI cycles before moving to IVF. Read our IUI vs IVF comparison guide to understand when to step up treatment.

Step by Step

IUI Process at Mother Hospitals

1

Initial Assessment

Hormone tests, semen analysis, and pelvic scan to confirm IUI is appropriate.

2

Ovulation Monitoring

Ultrasound scans track follicle growth. Trigger injection given when follicles are ready.

3

Sperm Preparation

Semen sample is washed and concentrated in the lab to select the best quality sperm.

4

Insemination

Prepared sperm is inserted directly into the uterus using a thin catheter. Takes 5–10 minutes.

5

Pregnancy Test

Blood test or urine pregnancy test 14 days later to confirm result.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How many IUI cycles should I try before IVF?+

Most fertility specialists recommend 3–6 IUI cycles before progressing to IVF, depending on age and diagnosis. Dr. E. Prashanthi Reddy will guide you based on your individual situation.

What is the IUI procedure cost at Mother Hospitals?+

IUI is significantly more affordable than IVF. Please call 97059 93366 or WhatsApp us for current pricing and package details.

Is IUI painful?+

IUI is a simple, relatively painless outpatient procedure. Some women may feel mild cramping. No anaesthesia is required.

What are the chances of twins with IUI?+

With stimulated IUI (using fertility drugs), there is a slightly higher chance of twins (5–15%) due to multiple follicle development. Your doctor will monitor closely to reduce this risk.

Can IUI be done with donor sperm?+

Yes. IUI can be performed using donor sperm at Mother Hospitals & IVF Center, in compliance with ART Act 2021 regulations.

How long does IUI take?+

The actual insemination takes only 5–10 minutes. You'll need to visit the clinic 2–3 times during your cycle for monitoring scans.
