What We Offer — Serving Uppal Patients

🔬 Complete RPL Investigation

Chromosomal karyotype (couple), thrombophilia screen (antiphospholipid antibodies, Factor V Leiden, prothrombin mutation), thyroid panel, uterine cavity evaluation (SIS/hysteroscopy).

🧬 PGT-A — Embryo Chromosomal Testing

The most common cause of miscarriage is embryo chromosomal abnormality. PGT-A (Preimplantation Genetic Testing) screens IVF embryos before transfer — only transferring chromosomally normal embryos, dramatically reducing miscarriage risk.

💊 Antiphospholipid Syndrome (APS)

Blood-clotting disorder causing recurrent miscarriage — treated with low-dose aspirin + low molecular weight heparin (LMWH / Clexane) from early pregnancy. Very effective when diagnosed.

🔍 Uterine Cause Evaluation

Uterine septum, submucous fibroids, polyps, Asherman's syndrome (intrauterine adhesions) — evaluated by HSG, SIS (saline ultrasound), or hysteroscopy.

FAQs — Uppal Patients

How many miscarriages before I see a specialist near Uppal?
Official guidelines say 3 or more, but increasingly specialists (including Dr. Prashanthi Reddy) recommend investigation after 2 miscarriages — especially in women over 35. There is no reason to wait for a third loss if a cause can be found and treated after the second.
What causes recurrent miscarriage?
In order of frequency: (1) Embryo chromosomal abnormality — most common; (2) Antiphospholipid syndrome (clotting disorder); (3) Uterine abnormalities (septum, fibroids, polyps); (4) Thyroid disorder; (5) Thrombophilia (Factor V Leiden, MTHFR); (6) Immune factors; (7) Male factor (sperm DNA fragmentation). Cause found in 50–60% of RPL cases.
What is PGT-A and how does it help recurrent miscarriage near Uppal?
PGT-A (Preimplantation Genetic Testing for Aneuploidy) biopsies IVF embryos at the blastocyst stage and tests all 23 chromosome pairs. Only chromosomally normal (euploid) embryos are transferred — reducing miscarriage risk from 20–30% per transfer to under 10%.
Can natural killer cells cause recurrent miscarriage near Uppal?
Natural killer (NK) cell testing is offered in the RPL workup. Elevated uterine NK cells are associated with recurrent implantation failure and miscarriage. Treatment includes intralipid infusion or prednisolone. Evidence is still evolving — Dr. Prashanthi advises on a case-by-case basis.
