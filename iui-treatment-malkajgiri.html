💉 IUI — Simple 3-Step Process

Step 1: Ovulation induction with tablets (Day 2–6). Step 2: Monitoring scan (Day 10–12) — confirm follicle ready. Step 3: HCG trigger → IUI procedure 36 hrs later. Sperm washed in lab, placed via fine catheter into uterus. 15 minutes. Home same day.

🎯 Who Is IUI Right For?

Trying for 1–2 years with no identified problem (unexplained). Mild male factor (count 10M+). PCOS — with ovulation induction. Cervical mucus issues. Borderline semen parameters. Open fallopian tubes confirmed.

📊 Success Rate — 20–30% Per Cycle

Natural IUI: 10–15%. Medicated IUI: 20–30%. 3 cycles are tried before moving to IVF. Under 35 with mild issues: best results. Dr. Prashanthi reviews after each cycle and advises honestly on whether to continue or escalate.

💰 Much Less Than IVF

IUI costs a fraction of IVF. No egg retrieval, no embryology lab fees, fewer medicines. Many couples avoid IVF entirely by succeeding in IUI. It is always worth trying first if you are eligible.

🔁 When to Move to IVF

After 3 IUI failures, or if: age 38+, tubes blocked, severe male factor, or PCOS not responding to induction. Dr. Prashanthi sets clear expectations at first consultation — no time wasted on unlikely options.

Frequently Asked Questions

How many visits are needed for IUI from Malkajgiri?
Per cycle: Day 2–3 baseline scan (1 visit) → Day 10–12 monitoring scan (1 visit) → IUI day (1 visit) → pregnancy test Day 14 (blood test, can be done at a local lab). Total: 3 clinic visits spread over 2 weeks. Very manageable.
Is IUI painful? Will I need to rest after?
IUI is not painful — mild cramping similar to period cramps for 15–30 minutes. No anaesthesia. You can go to work or home immediately after. Some prefer to rest 20 minutes at the clinic — perfectly fine either way.
Husband's count is 15 million — is IUI possible?
Yes. IUI is typically recommended when the washed motile count is 5–10 million+. 15 million total count usually gives a good washed count. Semen analysis will confirm. If husband's count is below 5M washed, IVF+ICSI is better.
PCOS + IUI — does it work?
Very well when combined with ovulation induction. Letrozole triggers one or two dominant follicles — giving a clear target for timed IUI. Success rate 20–30% per cycle for PCOS. 3 cycles before moving to IVF if needed.
Is IUI covered by insurance?
Some new-generation insurance policies (Star Women Care, Niva Bupa) offer limited fertility coverage. Check your policy. Mother Hospitals provides itemized billing to support any insurance claim you wish to make.
