FAQs — Bandlaguda Patients

How is IUI done?
Washed sperm is placed directly into the uterus via a thin catheter at ovulation. 5–10 minutes, painless, no anaesthesia. Return to work the same day.
How many IUI cycles before IVF?
Typically 2–3 cycles before escalating to IVF. For women over 35 or blocked tubes, Dr. Prashanthi may recommend IVF sooner.
What is the IUI success rate?
10–20% per cycle depending on age, diagnosis, and sperm parameters. With ovulation induction, success rates improve.
Is IUI painful?
No — generally painless. Mild cramping may occur. No anaesthesia needed. Return to normal activities the same day.
