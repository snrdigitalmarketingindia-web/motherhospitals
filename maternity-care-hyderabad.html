Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about maternity care at Mother Hospitals

What is the Mother 9 Antenatal Card?

The Mother 9 Antenatal Card is a comprehensive antenatal care programme at ₹500 per pregnancy — one card covers your entire pregnancy. It includes all OPD consultations with Dr. E. Prashanthi Reddy, routine blood tests, ultrasound scans at discounted rates, nutritional guidance, and antenatal education sessions — giving expectant mothers complete support from the first trimester through delivery.

Do you offer painless delivery (epidural) in Hyderabad?

Yes. Mother Hospitals offers epidural analgesia (painless delivery) as an option for expectant mothers who want to manage labour pain effectively. Our anaesthesiologist team is available round the clock to administer and monitor epidural pain relief safely throughout active labour.

What newborn care does Mother Hospitals provide after delivery?

Our experienced obstetric team provides comprehensive postnatal and newborn care — routine newborn check-ups, breastfeeding support, and postnatal recovery monitoring. Babies requiring specialist neonatal intensive care are promptly referred to specialist neonatal facilities to ensure the best possible outcomes.

Is Aarogyasri / EHS accepted for delivery at Mother Hospitals?

Yes. Mother Hospitals accepts Aarogyasri and EHS (Employee Health Scheme) for eligible deliveries and maternity care procedures. Please bring your health card at the time of admission. Our billing team will guide you through the paperwork and ensure a smooth cashless experience.

How do I book a maternity consultation at Mother Hospitals?

You can book a consultation by calling 97059 93366 or 97059 93355, or by sending a WhatsApp message to 90520 74999. Walk-in appointments are also welcome during clinic hours: every day (Monday to Sunday), 10:30 AM – 1:30 PM.

What is the cost of delivery at Mother Hospitals Hyderabad?

Delivery costs vary based on the type of delivery and any additional medical requirements. Normal (vaginal) deliveries start from ₹15,000 and are the most affordable option. C-section deliveries and high-risk pregnancy management are priced differently. The Mother 9 Antenatal Card — ₹500 per pregnancy — significantly reduces your overall antenatal care expenditure.
