Our Services

🩸 AMH Blood Test

Anti-Müllerian Hormone — most reliable marker of ovarian reserve. Done any day of the cycle, fasting not required. Result within 24–48 hours. AMH > 1.0: good reserve. 0.3–1.0: low. < 0.3: very low. Guides treatment intensity.

📡 Antral Follicle Count (AFC)

Transvaginal ultrasound on Day 2–3 of period. Counts visible small follicles in both ovaries. AFC > 12: normal. 6–12: low-normal. < 6: poor. Combined with AMH — best predictor of IVF response.

🧪 Hormone Panel

FSH, LH, Estradiol (Day 2–3). Prolactin. Thyroid (TSH, FT4). These assess pituitary-ovarian function, rule out hyperprolactinaemia and thyroid dysfunction — common, treatable causes of irregular cycles.

💧 Semen Analysis

Complete male fertility assessment — count, motility, morphology, volume, pH. WHO 2021 reference values. Result 2–4 hours. If abnormal: repeat + sperm DNA fragmentation test + hormone check for the husband.

🔭 HSG — Tube Test (if needed)

Hysterosalpingography checks if fallopian tubes are open. Done Day 7–10. Mild cramping. Quick outpatient procedure. If tubes are blocked, IVF is needed — HSG confirms this early, saving time.

📋 What Happens After Testing

Consultation with Dr. Prashanthi to review all results. Clear explanation of what each result means. Personalised treatment plan — may be as simple as timed intercourse, or may lead to IUI/IVF. No pressure. Honest advice.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a fertility test take in Boduppal?
First visit: 1.5–2 hours. Includes consultation, blood draw (AMH + hormones), and transvaginal scan (AFC). Semen sample can be given same day or on a follow-up. Results: blood tests 24–48 hrs, semen analysis 2–4 hrs same day.
When should I do AMH — any day?
AMH can be done any day of the menstrual cycle — it does not fluctuate like FSH/LH. No fasting needed. Ideal to do on the same day as the antral follicle count scan (Day 2–3) for a complete ovarian reserve picture.
What if my AMH is low?
Low AMH means fewer eggs remaining — but it doesn't mean pregnancy is impossible. DuoStim, Mini-IVF, mild protocols are available. Dr. Prashanthi will explain what's realistically possible based on your AMH and AFC combined.
Does my husband need to come for the fertility test?
Both partners ideally come together for the first visit — it saves time. Husband provides semen sample (at the clinic, private room available). Both results reviewed together. If husband can't come, schedule him separately.
