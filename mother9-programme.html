Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the Mother 9 Card? +

The Mother 9 Card is an exclusive antenatal care package by Mother Hospitals at just ₹500. It gives you 9 free OPD consultations, 50% discount on ultrasound scans, 10% off pharmacy and lab tests, free nutrition counselling and a free antenatal education session — covering your entire 9-month pregnancy journey.

How much does normal delivery cost at Mother Hospitals? +

Normal delivery at Mother Hospitals starts from ₹15,000 all-inclusive. C-section (caesarean) starts from ₹35,000. Tubectomy starts from ₹15,000. These are transparent package rates — no surprise bills.

Who can get the Mother 9 Card? +

Any pregnant woman — whether naturally conceived or through IVF — can enrol for the Mother 9 Card. It is best taken early in the first trimester to maximise benefits across all 9 months of pregnancy.

Is Mother Hospitals equipped for high-risk pregnancies? +

Yes. Mother Hospitals is fully equipped for high-risk pregnancies including twin pregnancies, gestational diabetes, hypertension, thyroid disorders and IVF pregnancies. Dr. Prashanthi Reddy has extensive experience managing complex obstetric cases with personalised monitoring.

Can IVF patients also get the Mother 9 Card? +

Absolutely. IVF pregnancies require more careful monitoring — the Mother 9 Card is ideal for IVF patients to ensure close follow-up throughout the pregnancy. Our team has specific expertise in managing IVF pregnancies including twin and high-risk cases.
