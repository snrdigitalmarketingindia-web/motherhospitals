FAQs — Uppal Patients

Why get a second IVF opinion near Uppal?
A failed IVF cycle is not proof that IVF cannot work for you. Often the stimulation protocol, timing, or laboratory conditions can be improved. Many patients who failed 2–3 cycles at one clinic succeed at another with a redesigned protocol. A second opinion costs little and can change everything.
What should I bring to an IVF second opinion near Uppal?
Bring all previous IVF records: stimulation protocol sheets, egg collection report, embryo development reports, embryo grading, transfer record, endometrial thickness at transfer, and any genetic testing done. The more records you bring, the more Dr. Prashanthi can identify what can be changed.
What is recurrent implantation failure (RIF)?
RIF is when good-quality embryos fail to implant after 2+ transfers. Causes include: abnormal uterus (polyps, fibroid, adhesions), thin or unreceptive endometrium, thrombophilia, NK cell overactivity, or chromosomally abnormal embryos. Dr. Prashanthi investigates all these causes.
Can I do IVF at Mother Hospitals after failing at another clinic near Uppal?
Yes — many patients at Mother Hospitals near Uppal had 1–3 failed cycles at other clinics before succeeding here. A redesigned protocol, different stimulation drugs, PGT-A embryo testing, or a different implantation preparation can make the difference.
