What is IVF?

Understanding IVF Treatment

IVF (In Vitro Fertilisation) is an assisted reproductive technology where eggs are fertilised by sperm outside the body, and the resulting embryo is transferred to the uterus. It is the most effective fertility treatment available today.

Who Needs IVF?

IVF is recommended for blocked fallopian tubes, severe male infertility, unexplained infertility, failed IUI cycles, endometriosis, low ovarian reserve, PCOS, and advanced maternal age.

Step by Step

IVF Process at Mother Hospitals

1

Initial Consultation & Tests

Blood tests (AMH, FSH, LH), semen analysis, pelvic ultrasound to assess your fertility baseline.

2

Ovarian Stimulation

Hormonal medications stimulate the ovaries to produce multiple eggs. Monitoring scans every 2–3 days.

3

Egg Pick-Up (OPU)

Eggs are retrieved under mild sedation using an ultrasound-guided needle. Takes 20–30 minutes.

4

Fertilisation (ICSI/IVF)

Eggs are fertilised in the IVF lab. Embryos develop over 3–5 days to blastocyst stage.

5

Embryo Transfer

The best quality embryo is transferred to the uterus. A simple, painless procedure — no anaesthesia needed.

6

Pregnancy Test

Beta HCG blood test 14 days after transfer confirms pregnancy.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the IVF success rate at Mother Hospitals Boduppal?+

Success rates depend on age and diagnosis. Dr. Prashanthi Reddy will give you an honest assessment during your first consultation. Patients under 35 with good ovarian reserve typically have the highest success rates.

How many days do I need to be in Hyderabad for IVF?+

A standard IVF cycle requires approximately 12–15 days in Hyderabad. The stimulation phase can be monitored locally, and you come to Hyderabad for egg pick-up and embryo transfer.

Is IVF painful?+

Modern IVF is largely painless. Egg pick-up is done under mild sedation. Mother Hospitals also offers Needleless IVF — a pain-free protocol that avoids conventional injections.

What is the IVF package cost at Mother Hospitals?+

The all-inclusive IVF package is ₹99,000/- valid till 30 June 2026. This includes stimulation injections, OPU, ICSI, consultations and scans — no hidden costs.

Does Mother Hospitals treat recurrent IVF failure?+

Yes. Dr. Prashanthi Reddy specialises in recurrent IVF failure. Investigations include ERA test, Laser Assisted Hatching, and protocol review to identify and treat the root cause.

Is IVF covered under insurance or Aarogyasri?+

IVF is generally not covered under Aarogyasri. Some private insurance policies are beginning to offer fertility coverage — check your policy. We can provide documentation for insurance claims.
