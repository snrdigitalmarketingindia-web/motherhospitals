What is IVF?

Understanding IVF Treatment

IVF (In Vitro Fertilisation) is an assisted reproductive technology where eggs are fertilised by sperm outside the body, and the resulting embryo is transferred to the uterus. It is the most effective fertility treatment available today.

Who Needs IVF?

IVF is recommended for blocked fallopian tubes, severe male infertility, unexplained infertility, failed IUI cycles, endometriosis, low ovarian reserve, PCOS, and advanced maternal age.

Step by Step

IVF Process at Mother Hospitals

1

Initial Consultation & Tests

Blood tests (AMH, FSH, LH), semen analysis, pelvic ultrasound to assess your fertility baseline.

2

Ovarian Stimulation

Hormonal medications stimulate the ovaries to produce multiple eggs. Monitoring scans every 2–3 days.

3

Egg Pick-Up (OPU)

Eggs are retrieved under mild sedation using an ultrasound-guided needle. Takes 20–30 minutes.

4

Fertilisation (ICSI/IVF)

Eggs are fertilised in the IVF lab. Embryos develop over 3–5 days to blastocyst stage.

5

Embryo Transfer

The best quality embryo is transferred to the uterus. A simple, painless procedure — no anaesthesia needed.

6

Pregnancy Test

Beta HCG blood test 14 days after transfer confirms pregnancy.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is IVF (In Vitro Fertilisation)?+

Short answer: IVF is a fertility treatment where eggs are fertilised outside the body and the embryo is placed into the uterus. Detailed: IVF involves ovarian stimulation, egg retrieval under sedation, laboratory fertilisation using ICSI, embryo culture for 3–5 days, and then embryo transfer into the uterus. It is the most effective fertility treatment available, with success rates varying by age and diagnosis. At Mother Hospitals, IVF is ₹99,000 all-inclusive.

What is the IVF success rate at Mother Hospitals Hyderabad?+

Short answer: Success rates vary by age, diagnosis, and number of embryos. General guidance: Women under 35 with good ovarian reserve typically have the highest success rates (40–60% per cycle internationally). Women over 40 or with low AMH have lower rates but can still succeed with personalised protocols. Dr. Prashanthi Reddy provides an honest individual success estimate during your first consultation.

How many days do I need to be in Hyderabad for IVF?+

Short answer: Approximately 12–15 days in Hyderabad for a standard IVF cycle. Breakdown: The ovarian stimulation phase (10–14 days) can be monitored by a local gynaecologist in your city. You need to be in Hyderabad for the egg retrieval (OPU) and embryo transfer — typically 5–7 days apart. NRI patients receive complete remote coordination support.

Is IVF painful? What is Needleless IVF?+

Short answer: Modern IVF is largely painless. Needleless IVF eliminates daily injections completely. Standard IVF: Egg retrieval is done under mild sedation — you feel nothing during the procedure. Needleless IVF: Mother Hospitals is one of the few clinics in Hyderabad offering Needleless IVF, where oral medications replace conventional daily injections. Ideal for patients with needle phobia or who want a gentler experience.

When should a couple consult a fertility specialist for IVF?+

Short answer: See a specialist if you've been trying to conceive for 1 year (under 35) or 6 months (over 35). See Dr. Prashanthi Reddy sooner if you have: Known PCOS, blocked tubes, low sperm count, endometriosis, prior IVF failure, irregular periods, or if you are over 38.
